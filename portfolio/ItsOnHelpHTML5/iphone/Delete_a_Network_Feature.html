Delete a Network Feature

You should be careful when deleting Published network features. When you delete a Published network feature, the network feature will be removed from the ItsOn server and from all embedded-client devices on your network, which could affect how devices interact with and connect to your network, which could in turn affect initiatives such as network management and cost reduction, and potentially prevent devices from connecting at all.
  1. Go to Policy -> Features.
  2. Find the feature in the list that you want to delete, and then click the corresponding Delete button  in the right column. (The buttons in the right column appear when you hover the mouse pointer over the row.)
  3. Click Confirm.
