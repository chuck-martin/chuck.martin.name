Policies: Service, Entitlement, Data Session, WiFi, Behavior, and Voice Accounting

Policies control how devices access your network and are used as the basis for different types of products. With ItsOn Service Design Center, you create, configure, and, circulate the following types of policies:

Policy Type Description
Service

There are 2 types of service policies:

  • Service policies for network features. You can add one or more service policy to a network feature for network management.
  • Service Ecommerce policies. A service eCommerce policy is used to define the service offered in a plan.
