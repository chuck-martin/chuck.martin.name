Sign In to ItsOn Smart Services

Because the ItsOn Service Design Center is a secure web-based application, you must sign in with the credentials you have been given to access it.

  1. Open your web browser (you should always be using the latest version) and navigate to the URL you have been given for ItsOn Service Design Center.
  2. Enter your username and password.
  3. Click Sign In.
If your system has multiple partners and your credentials enable you to access more than one of them, when you sign in, you will sign in to the partner from which you last signed out.
