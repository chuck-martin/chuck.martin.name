Add a WiFi Policy to a Network Feature

  1. Click Add Policy >
    • New Auto On
    • New Histogram
    • New Spot Check
  2. Click on the policy name to change it from the default name. (Give it a meaningful name. This will help you find the policy when you are choosing from lists of policies.)
  3. Configure the settings for the type of policy: Auto On, Histogram, or Spot Check.
  4. If you added a WiFi Auto On policy, configure the policy event.
