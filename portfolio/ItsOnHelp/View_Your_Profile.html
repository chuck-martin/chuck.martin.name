View Your Profile

Your profile contains basic information about you and the credentials you need to log in to ItsOn Smart Services, including:

  • Name: Your identity, comprised of first name and last name. This is different from your username, which uniquely identifies you and that you cannot change.
  • Password: Used when you sign in to verify your username.

To view your profile

  • In the upper right of any ItsOn Smart Services page, click your username, and then click Profile.
