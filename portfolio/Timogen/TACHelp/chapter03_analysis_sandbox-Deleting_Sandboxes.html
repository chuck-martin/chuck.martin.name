Deleting Sandboxes

A sandbox can be deleted by the owner only if:

· No resolution plan generation request is pending
· The resolution plan has not yet been executed
To delete a sandbox
1 In the Function drop-down list, select Business User.
2 Click the Analysis tab.
3 Click Sandboxes.
4 Click for the sandbox you want to delete.
5 In the confirmation dialog box, click OK.
