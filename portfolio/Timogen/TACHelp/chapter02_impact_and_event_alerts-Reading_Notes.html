Reading Notes

Notes are automatically generated for an alert when changes are made to an alert, changes such as a status change, a severity change, a resolver action, or a new resolver. The notes help you track an alert's progress.

Any user who has subscribed to an alert can read the notes for that alert.

To read an alert's notes
1 View the detail page of the alert that contains the notes you want to read. (See "Viewing Alerts".)
2 In the tabbed section at the bottom of the page (you may have to scroll down to see the tabbed section), click the Notes tab.

The notes are listed in chronological order with the newest on top.
