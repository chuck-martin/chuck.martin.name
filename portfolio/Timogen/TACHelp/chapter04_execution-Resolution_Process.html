Resolution Process

When the resolution process definition (resolution plan) is executed, it becomes a Resolution Process. The Resolution Process page shows:

· All your resolution processes and their current status
· Others' resolution processes containing tasks assigned to you

The Resolution Process page shows:

Field Name
Description
Name
The internal ID of the Resolution Process. Click on the link to see the list of tasks in this Resolution Process.
Nodes
The Impact Nodes that have been included in the scope of the Resolution Process.
Sandbox ID
The internal Sandbox ID in which this Resolution Process has been defined. Click on the link to see the Sandbox in more detail. A View Only mode of the Sandbox is displayed.
Sandbox Name
The name of the sandbox.
Time Created
The date and the time on which this Resolution Process was created.
Status
Hover your mouse over Detail to see the status of the tasks in the process. The pop-up shows the number of tasks that are not started, aborted, running, and completed.

In the Others' Resolution Pane, the name of the Process Owner (Sandbox Owner) is also shown.
