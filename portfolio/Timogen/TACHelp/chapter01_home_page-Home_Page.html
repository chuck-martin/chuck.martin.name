Home Page

Your Home page shows a summary of system events, such as your most recent Impact and Event Alerts, your most recently assigned Tasks, and your most recently accessed Sandboxes. It is the starting point for the Timogen Adaptive Controller system when you log on in or switch to the Business User function and contains tabs and links that take you to other areas.

This chapter contains the overview information about the information that can be on your Home page.:

· "Impact Alerts"
· "Event Alerts"
· "Tasks"
· "Collaboration Requests"
· "Sandboxes"

To configure what you see on your Home page, see "Configuring Home Page Sections".
