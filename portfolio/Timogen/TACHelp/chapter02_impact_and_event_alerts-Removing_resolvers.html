Removing resolvers

Only resolvers can remove roles from the resolvers list of an alert. Resolvers are remove by role.

To remove resolvers
1 View the detail page of the alert that contains the resolvers you want to remove. (See "Viewing Alerts".)
2 In the tabbed section at the bottom of the page (you may have to scroll down to see the tabbed section), click the Resolvers tab.
3 Select the checkbox for the resolver role(s) you want removed.
4 Click Remove.
