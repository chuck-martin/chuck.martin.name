Approving Tasks

Some tasks require approval before they can be completed. If they do, an Approve link will appear in the Actions column.

To approve a task
1 In the Functions drop-down list, select Business User.
2 Click the Execution tab, then click Tasks. The Tasks page appears.
3 In the Actions column of the My Tasks list, click an Approve link. The Approval dialog box appears.
4 Review the tasks details, optionally enter a note, then click Approve.
5 In the confirmation dialog box, click OK.
