Executing Resolution Plans

After you have reviewed and evaluated the Resolution Plan, you can execute the plan. When the Resolution Plan is executed, the status of the Sandbox changes to `View Only' and it cannot be modified. The result of the Resolution Plan is used to evaluate the impact alert and this plan is also used for future decision making activities.

To execute a Resolution Plan
1 View a sandbox. (See "Viewing Sandboxes".)
2 Click Execute Resolution Plan.
3 In the confirmation dialog box, click Yes.
