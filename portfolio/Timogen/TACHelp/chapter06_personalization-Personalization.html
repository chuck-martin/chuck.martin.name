Personalization

The Personalization tab is where you configure what information you'll see in other areas of the program.You can configure the following:

· "Configuring Home Page Sections"
· "Alert Subscriptions"
· "Impact Node Subscriptions"
· "Search and Filter List Defaults"
· "Scheduled Report Subscriptions"
· "Profile"
