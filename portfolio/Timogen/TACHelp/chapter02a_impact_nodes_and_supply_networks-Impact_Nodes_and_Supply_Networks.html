Impact Nodes and Supply Networks

Impact Nodes

The Impact Nodes pages lists all the Impact Nodes you are subscribed to. (See "Impact Node Subscriptions".) An Impact Node is the combination of a single item and a single location. When an item is mapped to a specific location, an Impact Node implicitly exists for that item/location.

Impact Nodes allow you to recognize shortage or overstock conditions for a specific item at a specific location even as multiple events are changing in the supply chain. "Alert" appears in the Status column of the Impact Node list if an alert is currently in effect for that Impact Node.

Before alerts can occur for Impact Nodes, the Business Admin user must define the alert definition for that Impact Node. See "Alert Definition".
