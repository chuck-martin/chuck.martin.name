Evaluating Resolution Plans

You can validate the current Resolution Plan for execution feasibility.

To evaluate a Resolution Plan
1 View a sandbox. (See "Viewing Sandboxes".)
2 Click Evaluate Resolution Plan. The Resolution Plan Validation Result dialog box appears.

The Resolution Plan Validation Result list shows a list of Impact Nodes in the sandbox scope and the condition of each. Click on an Impact Node link to see the Supply/Demand view for that Impact Node.
