Impact Alerts

Impact Alerts indicate a shortage or overstock on an Impact Node. Each Impact Node can have only one Impact Alert, so Impact Alerts handle both overstocks and shortages simultaneously. All the Impact Alerts that you have subscribed to and are responsible for are displayed on the Impact Alert list page. From this page you can:

· View individual alerts in greater detail
· Archive alerts
· View or create sandboxes

For information on subscribing to Impact Alerts, see "Alert Subscriptions".
