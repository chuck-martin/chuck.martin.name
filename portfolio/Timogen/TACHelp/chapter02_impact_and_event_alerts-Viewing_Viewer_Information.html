Viewing Viewer Information

The Viewers tab lists all users who are authorized to view the alert and the last time each user has viewed it (or N/A if not viewed by the user).

To view viewer information
1 View the detail page of the alert that contains the viewers you want to view. (See "Viewing Alerts".)
2 In the tabbed section at the bottom of the page (you may have to scroll down to see the tabbed section), click the Viewers tab.

The list shows the ID, name, and last time viewed for each user authorized to view the alert.
