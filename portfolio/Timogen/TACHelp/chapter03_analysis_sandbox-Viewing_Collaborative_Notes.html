Viewing Collaborative Notes

Collaborative Notes are viewed on the Sandbox detail page.

To view a collaborative note
1 View a sandbox. (See "Viewing Sandboxes".)
2 Click the Collab. Notes tab in the lower pane.
3 Click a Subject link.
