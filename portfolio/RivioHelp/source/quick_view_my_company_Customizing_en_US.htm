Customizing the Quick View page

The  Quick View page always shows company announcements, but can also show:

  • Message of the Day (MOTD)
  • Job postings
  • Employee locator
  • Job rules

To customize the  Quick View page

  1. Click  .
  2. Click Customize this page.
  3. Select the options you want to appear in all Quick View pages and clear the options you do not want to appear.
  4. Click Save & Return.
