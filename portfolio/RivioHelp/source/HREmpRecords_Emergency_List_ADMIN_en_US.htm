The Emergency Contacts list shows you a summary of the employee's Emergency Contacts. The summary includes the relationships, phone numbers, and whether they are dependents of the employee. From this list you can add or modify details for an individual emergency contact.

What do you want to do?

Add an Emergency Contact
View or modify the information about an existing Emergency Contact
Delete one or more of the Emergency Contact entries in the list

To add an Emergency Contact

  1. Click , then under Employee Records, click Employee Info.
  2. Click Emergency.
  3. Click Add Record.
  4. Enter the information on the Emergency Contacts Information form.
  5. Click Save & Return.

To view or modify the information about an existing Emergency Contact

  • Click the Emergency Contact's Name.

To delete one or more of the Emergency Contact entries in the list

  1. Select the check box Emergency Contact(s) you want to delete.
  2. Click Delete.
