Adding an Invoice

To add an invoice

  1. On the Quick View or the Tasks page, from the drop-down list in the Task List section, select Invoice, then click Add.
    -or-
    Click My Work, then under To Do, click Invoices, then click Add Invoice.
  2. Enter the information for the invoice.
    The invoice number is automatically generated.
    The billing date and payment date are automatically entered, with payment due one week after billing date. You can change these dates.
    1. Select a customer from the Customer drop-down list. The Bill To, Address, and Payment Terms fields will be filled in with that customer's information.
    2. Enter a description in the Description box to identify this invoice.
    3. For each invoice item, enter an item description, quantity/hours, rate, and whether the item is taxable.
      To add additional items, click Add Item or Add 5 Items.
  3. Click Calculate.
  4. Click Save & Return.
