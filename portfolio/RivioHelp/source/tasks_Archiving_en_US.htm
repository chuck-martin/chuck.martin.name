Archiving a Task

You can archive any type of task so that it doesn't appear on your active desktop lists. Archived tasks are always available in the Archived tab of each task type. You can reopen archived tasks to make them active again.

Note: Some tasks, such as time sheets, cannot be archived until they have been sent for approval.

To archive a task

  • In any list of tasks, select the check box next to the task name, then click Archive.
    -or-
    Open the task, then click Archive.
