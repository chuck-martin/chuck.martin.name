Modifying Policies

You must have HR Administrator or System Administrator access rights to modify a policy.

To modify a policy

  1. Click  , then under Company Policies, click Setup.
  2. Click Policies.
  3. Click the title of the policy you want to modify.
  4. Enter your modifications.
  5. Optionally, you can select the checkbox to notify employees of this new policy by e-mail.
  6. Click Save & Return.
