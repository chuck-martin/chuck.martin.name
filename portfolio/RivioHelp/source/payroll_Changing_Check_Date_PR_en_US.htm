Changing a Payroll Check Date

To allow time for processing, a payroll's check date (the date that will appear on the paychecks) must be 2 business days past the day that the payroll is approved.

You defined a check date when you originally created the payroll. However, if either processing or approval is delayed, the original check date may be fewer than 2 days after approval.

To change a payroll check date

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Processing.
  2. In the In Progress area, click the payroll date range.
    If there are no longer 2 business days between now and the check date, you will see a message on the Calculated Results page.
  3. Enter a new check date that is at least 2 days from now.
  4. Click Re-submit.
