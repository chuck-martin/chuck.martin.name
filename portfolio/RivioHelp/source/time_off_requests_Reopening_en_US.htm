Reopening a Time-Off Request

If you need to make changes to your time-off request after it has been submitted for approval, you can make the changes by reopening it.

To reopen a time-off request

  1. On the Time-Off Requests page, click the link for a time-off request with a status of Pending Approval or Approved.
  2. Click Reopen.

Note: Once you reopen a time-off request, it is reset to Open status and is no longer submitted for approval.
