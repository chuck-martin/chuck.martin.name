About Time-Off Requests

In the Time-Off area, you can request and manage time-off of all types and durations. The features include:

  • Online preparation and routing of time-off requests.
  • E-mail notification of time-off request submission and status changes.
  • Convenient Time-Off Request display, allowing for easy management and status tracking of open time-off requests, as well as easy access to approved time off.
  • History log audit trail, helping to ensure the proper company time-off request approval process has been followed.
  • Auto-synchronization with your system calendar and the employee locator function.

Several predefined time-off types are available:

  • Vacation
  • Personal Day
  • Illness
  • Holiday
  • Jury Duty
  • Short Term Disability
  • Long Term Disability
  • Maternity
  • Compassionate
  • Sabbatical
  • Paid Time Off
  • Leave of Absence
