Enabling QuickBooks Integration

The Accounting area operates in two modes: with QuickBooks integration enabled or disabled.

To enable QuickBooks integration

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Setup.
  2. Click Set QuickBooks Integration Preferences.
  3. Click On.
  4. Select the types of data you want to import and export.
  5. Click Save & Return.
