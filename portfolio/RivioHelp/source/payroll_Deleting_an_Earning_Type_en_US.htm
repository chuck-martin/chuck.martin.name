Deleting an Earning Type

Earning types are the types of hours that you pay for, such as regular time, overtime, time-and-a-half, double time, sick time, personal time off, salary, and so on. You can easily delete an earning type that you no longer need.

To delete an earning type

  • Click   , then under Payroll, click Setup.
  • Click Earnings.
  • Click the Delete button next to the earning type you want to delete.
  • Click Save & Return.

Note: For InterPay users, once you define your original earning types and the InterPay processing setup is complete (typically 7-12 days from the date this information is sent to InterPay), these fields become non-editable. If you need to make changes after this, contact InterPay directly.
