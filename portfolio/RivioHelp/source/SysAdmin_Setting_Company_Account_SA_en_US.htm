The Company Account is the billing information we need for any monthly subscription charges and per-user charges based on your use of this system. Additional charges are incurred for additional users who are given sign-in access and for the total number of employee records that you maintain. This is completely separate from any charges made in the Goods and Services area.

To set the company credit account

  1. Enter the card number, expiration date, cardholder's name, and billing address to verify your authority to use this card.
  2. Click Save.
