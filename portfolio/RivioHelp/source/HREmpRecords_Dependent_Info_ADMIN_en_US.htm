The Dependent Information page records information about the employee's dependents. It may be seen and modified only by the employee or an HR Administrator.

The Comment area lets you or an HR Administrator make notes that are only visible to you or someone with HR Administrator privileges.

What do you want to do?

Use the same Home Address as the employee
Identify the dependent as an Emergency Contact
Delete this entire entry

To use the employee's Home Address for this dependent

  1. Select the Use same home address as employee check box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To identify this dependent as an Emergency Contact

  1. Click the Emergency Contact box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To delete this entire entry

  • Click Delete.
