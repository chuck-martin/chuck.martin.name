Setting up the Accounting Area

The Accounting Setup page controls three important areas of the Accounting area's operation:

  • QuickBooks Interface control
  • Expense Report and Invoice Document Serial Number Sequencing
  • Master Import and Export Data List Selection

What do you want to do?

Set the starting expense report and/or invoice number
Turn the QuickBooks Interface on or off
Control what data can be imported or exported
