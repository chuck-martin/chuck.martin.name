About Policies and Procedures

The Company Policies area is where you publish your organization's policies and procedures. Policies might include your standards for items such as Expense Report Reimbursements, Benefits, or Travel. Procedures give employees details on your organization's procedures, such as Security and Locking Up, What to do in Emergencies, or How to Contact Building Maintenance.

Any employee with access to the system can view policies. However, you must have Administrator access rights to create or modify the policies.
