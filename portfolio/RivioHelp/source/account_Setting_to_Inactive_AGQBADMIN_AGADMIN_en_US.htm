Setting an Account to Inactive

Accounts can have a status of Active or Inactive. You can then filter the list of accounts so that inactive accounts don't appear in the list, making the list easier to manage.

To set an account to inactive

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Account List.
  2. Select an account that has a status of Active.
    The status appears in the Account Status box when you select the account.
  3. Click View Selected.
  4. Select Inactive.
  5. Click Save.
