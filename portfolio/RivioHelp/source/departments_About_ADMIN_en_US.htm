About Departments

The Department List includes your company's departments, and displays the department name, ID, and number of members. If you are using the payroll feature, the payroll reference number also displays.

What do you want to do?

Add a new department
Deactivate a department
Change the name or ID of a department
See which employees are in a specific department
See all employee department assignments
Move employees to a new department
See employees not assigned to a department
