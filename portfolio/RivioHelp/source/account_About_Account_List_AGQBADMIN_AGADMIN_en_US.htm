About the Account List

The Account List shows you a list of accounts and sub-accounts (your Chart of Accounts). From here you can view, create, modify, and delete accounts and sub-accounts. Accounts are used to categorize expenses in your expense reports.

The Account List is available only if you have QuickBooks integration turned off.

What do you want to do?

View account information
Add an account
Add a sub-account
Modify an account
Hide inactive accounts
Show all accounts
Set an account to active status
Set an account to inactive status
