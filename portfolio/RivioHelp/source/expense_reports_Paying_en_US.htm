Paying an Expense Report

Expense reports with a status of Approved for Payment have been approved by a manager and sent to you for payment.

To pay an expense report

  1. On the Expense Reports page, click the link of the expense report that has the Approved for Payment status.
  2. Click Accept for Payment.

Note: For QuickBooks users, the expense report is now ready for export to QuickBooks. The expense report will be exported to QuickBooks the next time that the Accounting Administrator uses this feature.
