About My Personal Profile

My Personal Profile is divided into six parts. To open each part, click the corresponding tab.

  • Personal: This records your personal information and personal contact information.
  • Work: This displays information about your job and allows you to change information that others can see about how to contact you at work.
  • Employment: This displays your employment status.
  • Dependents: This gives you a list of your dependents. From here you may change the details about any dependent.
  • Emergency: This provides a list of your emergency contacts. From here you may change the details about any emergency contact.
  • Pay Rate: This displays your compensation information including base pay, deductions, withholdings, and direct deposit.
