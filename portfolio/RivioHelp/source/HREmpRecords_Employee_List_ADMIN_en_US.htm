Using the Employee Records List

The Employee Records List shows you list of all employees.

When you first open the list, you see all employees. You may change the contents of the list to show only active employees, or use the search-filter to limit the list to a single department or name.

What do you want to do?

Add an employee record
Include an employee in the payroll list
Include non-active employees in the list
Search for one or more employees
Modify the information for a specific employee

To include non-active employees in the list

  • Clear the Show Only Active Employees check box.
