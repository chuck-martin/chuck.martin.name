To see which employees are in a specific department

  1. Click the department name. You see a list of all employees in that department.
  2. To edit an employee's record, click the employee name.

To see all employee department assignments

Click the Employees in All Departments link.

To see employees not assigned to a department

  1. Click the Employees in All Departments link. Employees who are not assigned to a department will be shown as Unassigned.
  2. To group all Unassigned employees together, click the Department ID column heading.
