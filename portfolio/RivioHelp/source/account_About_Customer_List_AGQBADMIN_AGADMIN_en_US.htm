About the Customer List

The Customer List contains your customers and projects and is always available. Projects are associated with customers.

When QuickBooks integration is enabled, you can import your customer and project records from QuickBooks and use that data throughout the system. You can also create new customers and projects. These changes can then be exported to QuickBooks to keep both systems synchronized.

What do you want to do?

View customer or project information
Add a customer
Add a project
Modify a customer
Modify a project
Hide inactive customers and projects
Show all customers and projects
Set a customer or project to active status
Set a customer or project to inactive status
