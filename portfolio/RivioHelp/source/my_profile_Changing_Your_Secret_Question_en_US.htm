Changing Your Secret Question

The secret question (and its answer) is used by customer service if you forget your password to help verify that it's you who is calling. The question should be something about you and the answer should be something that only you would know.

To change your secret question

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Password.
  2. Enter a new secret question.
  3. Enter an answer to that question.
  4. Click Save.
