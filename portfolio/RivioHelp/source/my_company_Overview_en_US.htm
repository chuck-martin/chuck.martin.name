My Company Overview

Under the  tab, you can find and communicate up-to-date information about your company. The different areas of the left menu each contain different types of information, such as company announcements, locations, events, an org chart, and more.

Area

Contents

Profile

General information about the company, such as the headquarters address, phone number, e-mail addresses, Web site, and other company locations.

Events

A calendar of company-wide events.

Organization Chart

Chart that shows the organization hierarchy.

Job Roles

Listing of the roles and responsibilities of each job within the company.

Employee Directory

Listing of all employees and basic information (such as phone and e-mail) of each.

Employee Locator

Listing of employees who have marked themselves out of the office, with contact information if provided.

Job Postings

Listing of open jobs within the company.

Departments

Listing of departments within the company.
