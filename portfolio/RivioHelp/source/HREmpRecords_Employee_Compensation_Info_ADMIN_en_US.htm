Use the Pay Rate tab to record the employee's compensation information. This information will be available to the HR Administrator and may be seen but not modified by the employee.

The Comment area lets you make notes that are only visible to users with HR Administrator access rights.

What do you want to do?

Set the expiration date for a pay rate
View a history of the employee's pay rates

To set the expiration date for a pay rate

Set a new pay rate.

A pay rate expires when a new pay rate is established. The current pay rate will have no expiration date. When you View All Records, you will see the expiration date for an historical pay rate, which is the date a new rate became effective.

To view a history of the employee's pay rates

When you're in the Current Compensation Record page, click View All Records.
