Customer Detail page

Use the following information to enter the correct information on the Customer Detail page when you are adding or modifying a customer.

Item

Description

Customer Detail

The Customer Name is required and is what appears in the Customer List.

The Status can be Active or Inactive; you can hide Inactive customers in the Customer List.

Contact Information

Enter optional contact information for the customer, including name, e-mail address, phone and fax numbers, and billing and shipping addresses. For Country, you can choose U.S.A. or Outside U.S.A.

Payment Information

For Payment Terms, if QuickBooks integration is disabled, choose from the default list of 1% 10 NET 30, 2% 10 NET 30, Due on receipt, NET 15, NET 30, or NET 60. If QuickBooks integration is enabled, choose from the payment terms you established in QuickBooks.

For Resale number, if a customer is sometimes exempt from sales tax, enter the customer's tax exemption ID.

For Tax item, enter the tax you usually charge this customer (or enter a non-taxable item if you don't charge this customer sales tax). A tax item represents a single tax that you collect at a specified rate and pay to a single agency.
