Modifying Direct Deposit

Use the Direct Deposit tab of Payroll Information to modify your current Direct Deposit information. This form does not automatically start Direct Deposit. It lets you enter the information so that your Payroll Administrator has everything needed to start Direct Deposit.

What do you want to do?

Specify details for your bank account
Start automatic payroll deposit

To specify details for your bank account

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click Direct Deposit.
  3. Enter the appropriate information.
  4. Click Save & Return.

To start automatic payroll deposit

Contact your Payroll Administrator.
