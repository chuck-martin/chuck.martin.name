About Job Roles

The Job Roles page makes it easy to identify who does what within the company, even when frequent shifting of roles change employees' job responsibilities.

Click My Company > Company Info > Job Roles to view employees' names, titles, and brief descriptions of their jobs. You can sort the list by name or title, and you can search the job description text for key words.
