To move employees to a different department

  1. Click the employees' current department or click Employees in All Departments.
  2. Find the employees' names and click the selection box to the left of each name.
  3. Choose the new department from the drop-down list.
  4. Click Transfer to move all selected employees.
  5. If you select the special department name New, you are taken to the Add Department form.
  6. Click Save & Return on that form to create the department and move the selected employees to that department.
