About Company Events

Company events are calendar items that apply to everyone in the company. You can set up the system so that company events automatically appear on everyone's calendars. Company events can be of several types, such as:

  • Holidays
  • Company meetings
  • Company parties/celebrations

You must have System Administrator or Human Resources Administrator access rights to add, change, or delete company events.
