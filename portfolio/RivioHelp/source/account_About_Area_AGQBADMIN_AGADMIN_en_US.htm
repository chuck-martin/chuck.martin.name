About the Accounting Area

The Accounting area provides two different ways of supporting your accounting procedures:

  • Integration with QuickBooks Pro® 99, 2000, or 2001, where you can share several types of data between your QuickBooks software and this system (by enabling QuickBook integration).
  • Standalone management of your account list (by disabling QuickBook integration).
