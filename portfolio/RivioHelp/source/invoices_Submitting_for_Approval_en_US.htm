Submitting an Invoice for Approval

Submitting an invoice for approval can be done for two different reasons:

  • If you've created an invoice and your company requires it to be approved before it is sent to a client.
  • If you're a client company and you've received an invoice and it requires approval before payment.

To submit an invoice for approval before sending to a client

  1. Open an invoice with a status of New or Open.
  2. Click Submit for Approval.
  3. In the Select Name drop-down list box, select the name of an authorized user.
    -or-
    In the E-mail Address box, type a valid e-mail address.
    Note: Only employees who have been assigned Manager access rights appear in the drop-down list.
  4. If desired, type an additional comment or Take Action by date.
  5. Click Send.

If you're a client and you received an e-mail that says you've been sent an Invoice, the invoice contains a link to the Invoices area with a guest account that will let you access and work with that invoice.

To submit for approval an invoice you've received

  1. Click on the link in the e-mail you received. Your default browser will open to the sign-in page with the Company ID and User ID field already filled in.
  2. In the Password field, type "password" (without the quotes).
  3. Click Sign-In.
  4. Click Submit For Approval.
  5. In the E-mail Address box, enter the e-mail address of the person who must approve the invoice.
  6. Click Send.
