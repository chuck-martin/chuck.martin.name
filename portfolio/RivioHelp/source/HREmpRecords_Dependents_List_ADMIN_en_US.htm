The Dependents list shows a summary of the employee's dependents. The summary includes the relationship, primary phone number, and whether they are an emergency contact for the employee. From this list you can add or modify details for an individual dependent.

What do you want to do?

Add a dependent
View or modify the information about an existing dependent
Delete one or more of the dependent entries in the list

To add a dependent

  1. Click , then under Employee Records, click Employee Info.
  2. Click Dependents.
  3. Click Add Record.
  4. Enter the information about the dependent.
  5. Click Save & Return.

To view or modify the information about an existing dependent

  1. Click the name of the dependent in the Dependent column.
  2. To modify, make any desired changes, then click Save & Return.

To delete one or more of the dependent entries in the list

  1. Select the check box of the dependent(s) you want to delete.
  2. Click Delete.
