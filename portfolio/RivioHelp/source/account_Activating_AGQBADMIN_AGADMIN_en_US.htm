Activating Accounts

If you think you may have wanted to use accounts again, you would have deactivated them instead of deleting them. To use those accounts again for your business, you'll need to activate them.

To activate an account

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Account List.
  2. Click Show All.
  3. Select the inactive account, then click View Selected.
    When you select an account, its status, Active or Inactive, appears in the Accoutn Status box.
  4. Select Active.
  5. Click Save.
