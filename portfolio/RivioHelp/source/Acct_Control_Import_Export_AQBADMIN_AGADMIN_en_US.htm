Controlling What Data can be Imported or Exported

When the QuickBooks® interface is on, you can exchange a variety of data between your QuickBooks system and this system.

On the Accounting Setup page are two lists: items that you can import and items you can export. The check boxes in these lists are the master control over what can be imported or exported. At the time you perform an import or export, you can further limit what information you want to use, but you cannot import or export any items unless permission is given here.

The import and export lists are not identical. Some items may only be imported from your QuickBooks into this system, some may only be exported from this system to your QuickBooks system, and some can go both ways.

  • Customer List: Your customer list can be both imported and exported.
  • Employee List: Your employee list can be both imported and exported.
  • Chart of Accounts: Your Chart of Accounts or Account List can only be imported from your QuickBooks system into this system.
  • Credit Terms: Your Credit Terms can only be imported from your QuickBooks system into this system.
  • Expense Reports: Your Expense Reports can only be exported from this system to your QuickBooks system.

To limit export or import of data

  1. Click the checkbox(es) to un-check any items you wish to block from import or export.
  2. Click Save.
