When you add a department, it becomes available for employees to be assigned to it on the Employee Records, Work tab. Department names or IDs are also used for Payroll accounting.

To add a new department

  1. Click , then under  Info, click Departments.
  2. Click Add Department
  3. Enter the department name and ID. If you are using the payroll feature, enter the payroll reference number also.
  4. Click Save & Return to save your information.
