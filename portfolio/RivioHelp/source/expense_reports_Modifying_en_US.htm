Modifying an Expense Report

You can modify a New or Open expense report if you need to add, delete, or change the details of expense items, or change other items, such as the advance amount or summary comment.

To add an expense report item

  1. On the Quick View or Expense Reports page, click an expense report. (Only Open expense reports that have not been forwarded to someone else can be modified.)
  2. Add the item information in the New Expense Items section.
    For each expense item:
    1. Enter the date of the expense.
    2. Select the expense type.
    3. Enter the expense amount.
    4. Select the customer/project that the expense is related to (if applicable).
    5. Enter a note to describe the expense in more detail (optional).
    6. Click the Details link to:
      • Convert foreign currency to U.S. dollars
      • Identify an expense item as company-paid
      • Note that a receipt is not available
  3. Click Save & Return.

Click Submit for Approval when the expense report is completed to submit the expense report to the appropriate person and receive your reimbursement.

To delete an expense report item

  1. On the Quick View or Expense Reports page, click the link for an expense report.
  2. Click Delete next to the item you want to delete.
  3. Click Save & Return.

To change the details of an expense report item

  1. On the Quick View or Expense Reports page, click the link for an expense report.
  2. Click Detail next to the item you want to change.
  3. On the Expense Report Item Detail page, make any desired changes.
  4. Click Save.
  5. Click Close.
  6. Click Save & Return.

Click Submit for Approval when the expense report is completed to submit the expense report to the appropriate person and receive your reimbursement.
