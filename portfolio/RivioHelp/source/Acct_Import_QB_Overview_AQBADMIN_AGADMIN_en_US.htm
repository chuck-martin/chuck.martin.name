Import Data from QuickBooks Overview

Because Accounting and Human Resources are separate administrative privileges, whenever anyone with Accounting Administrator access imports Employee Records, e-mail is automatically sent to all HR Administrators notifying them of the records that have been imported. This is a summary with a count of the records, not the details.

You can import Payment Terms from QuickBooks. After the data file is imported, those terms are available in the Customer Detail screen.

The Accounts List and Customer List are completely compatible with the QuickBooks version; you can have parent accounts and up to four levels of child accounts under each parent. If you have a deeply structured list in your QuickBooks, that will be preserved when you import it into our system.
