Archiving a Payroll

You can archive payrolls to keep the Current or Completed payrolls pages short and manageable. Archived payrolls are always available for viewing and printing.

To archive a payroll

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Processing.
  2. In the Payroll Periods section, select the check box next to a payroll
    -or-
    click Completed, then select the check box next to a payroll.
  3. Click Archive.

That payroll is now listed under the Archived tab.
