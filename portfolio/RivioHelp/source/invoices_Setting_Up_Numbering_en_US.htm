Setting Up Invoice Numbering

Every invoice you generate should have a unique identifier. In the Invoices area, you can generate sequential numbers automatically for invoices. You can choose a starting number, and add a prefix or a suffix to the number.

To set up invoice numbering

  1. In  , click Accounting, then Setup.
  2. Click Set Expense Report and Invoice Sequencing.
  3. Enter the desired setup information. (The prefix and suffix are static; they don't increment).
  4. Click Save & Return.
