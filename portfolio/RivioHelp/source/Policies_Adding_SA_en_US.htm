Adding Policies

You must have HR Administrator or System Administrator access rights to add a policy.

To add a policy

  1. Click , then under Company Policies, click Setup.
  2. Click Policies.
  3. Click Add Policy.
  4. Enter the Policy Name.
  5. Under Policy Content, select Upload File From Disk or Enter Text.
    • For Upload File From Disk, click the Upload File From Disk link and enter the full path to the file, or click Browse to locate the file. Then click Upload.
      Note: Files must be Microsoft Word (.DOC) or Rich Text Format (.RTF) files. Each file can be no more than 100 KB, and the total size of all Procedure files uploaded cannot exceed 1 MB.
    • For Enter Text, enter text or simple HTML.
  6. Optionally, you can select the checkbox to notify employees of this new policy by e-mail.
  7. Click Save & Return.
