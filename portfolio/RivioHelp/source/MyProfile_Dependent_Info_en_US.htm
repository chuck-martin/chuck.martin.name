The Dependent Information form records information about your dependents. It may be seen and modified only by you or an HR Administrator.

What do you want to do?

Use your Home Address for this dependent
Identify this dependent as an Emergency Contact
Delete this entire entry

To use your Home Address for this dependent

  1. Select the Use same home address as employee check box.
  2. Click Save. This will automatically copy the home address information from your personal information.

To identify this dependent as an Emergency Contact

  1. Select the Emergency Contact check box.
  2. Click Save. This will automatically copy this dependent information into your Emergency Contacts list.

To delete this entire entry

Click Delete.
