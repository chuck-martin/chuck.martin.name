Entering Hours on a Time Sheet

Use a time sheet to track not only your work hours, but your time off hours.

To enter hours on a time sheet

  1. On the Time Sheets page, click the link for a time sheet.
  2. Enter your hours in the appropriate category and for the appropriate date.
    Time entry covers time worked (by client and project, if applicable) and time off.
  3. Click Calculate Totals.
  4. Click Save & Return.
