Resetting a Password

When you reset an employee's password, a new, random password is generated.

You must have System Administrator access rights to modify another employee's password.

To modify a password

  1. Click  , then under System Admin, click Access Rights.
  2. Click Notify Employee/Change Access Rights.
  3. Click a name.
  4. Click Reset Password.
  5. Click Save.

An e-mail is sent notifying the employee of the new password.
