About System Admin

The System Admin area includes those features that control your company's overall use of this system. This includes who in your company has access to the system, and what parts of the system they can access. It also includes the details of how your company is billed for system-related charges.

  • Access Rights: Use this to specify who in your company has sign-in access and the areas for which they have access permission.
  • Account Info: Here you can view your plan information, view your billing and payment information, and view or modify your credit card information.
  • System Setup: Use this for access to all the setup options of the other parts of the system.
