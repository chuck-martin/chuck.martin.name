Adding Deduction Types

This page lists the descriptions of all the deductions your company uses on its payroll.

To add a deduction type

  1. Click  , then under Payroll, click Setup.
  2. Click Deductions.
  3. Click Add.
  4. Enter a name (Integrated Payroll Services will use just the first four characters), a text description, and a unique reference number.
  5. Click Save & Return.

Note: For InterPay users, once you define your original deduction types and the InterPay processing setup is complete (typically 7-12 days from the date this information is sent to InterPay), these fields become non-editable. If you need to make changes after this, contact InterPay directly.
