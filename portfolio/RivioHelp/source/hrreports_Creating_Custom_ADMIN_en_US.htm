Crating a Custom Report

In addition to the availalbe pre-defined reports, you can create custom reports from any combination of employee information.

To create a custom report

  1. Click , then under Employee Records, click Reports.
  2. Click Custom Export.
  3. Select the employees for the report, then click Next.
    The default is all employees selected.
  4. Select the information you want included in the report for each employee.
  5. Click Export.

You can then choose to open the report, in either Microsoft Excel or whatever program you have defined as the default for opening spreadsheet files, or to save the report to you hard disk.
