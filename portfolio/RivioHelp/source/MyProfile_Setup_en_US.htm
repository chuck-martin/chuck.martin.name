Modifying My Profile Setup

My Profile Setup allows you to specify your personal options to receive information and your personal calendar options.

What do you want to do?

Opt-in or opt-out of receiving announcements, surveys, and/or newsletter
Specify whether Company Events, Tasks Due, and/or Time-Off Requests are included in your personal calendar

To opt-in or opt-out of receiving announcements, surveys, and/or newsletter

  1. Select the appropriate check boxes.
  2. Click Save.

To specify items included in your personal calendar

  1. Select the appropriate check boxes.
  2. Click Save.
