Adding a Purchase Request

To add a purchase request

  1. On the Quick View or the Tasks page, from the drop-down list in the Task List section, select Purchase Request, then click Add.
    -or-
    On the Purchase Requests page, click Add Purchase Request.
  2. Enter a purpose in the Purpose field to identify this purchase request.
  3. Enter the information for this purchase request.
  4. Enter a date required, information about a preferred vender, a tracking number, and a comment (all optional).
  5. For each purchase order item, enter an item description, a quantity and a unit cost, and select an optional customer reference for the purchase.
  6. Click Save & Return.

Note: Saving a purchase report does not submit it for approval.
