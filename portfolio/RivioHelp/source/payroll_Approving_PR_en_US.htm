Approving a Payroll

When InterPay finishes calculating results of a payroll you've submitted, they return a summary to you (viewable from this page) for approval before checks will be cut.

To approve a payroll

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Processing.
    If your payroll calculated results are ready for review and approval, the Status in the In Progress area will read "Calculations Received."
    Note: If the status is "Calculating," you can click Check for Results in a few minutes to see if the calculated results are ready for your approval.
  2. Click the date range for the payroll.
  3. Review the calculated results summary.
  4. Click Approve.
