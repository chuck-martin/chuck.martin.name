Approving an Expense Report

Expense reports with a status of Pending Approval have been sent to you for approval. After you approve the expense report, it is sent on for payment to authorized users (those with manager access rights) or to anyone with a valid e-mail address.

To approve an expense report

  1. On the Expense Reports page, click the link of the expense report that has the Pending Approval status.
  2. Click Approve and Submit for Payment.
  3. Using the Send To drop-down list, select the name of a user.
    -or-
    In the E-mail box, type a valid e-mail address.
  4. If desired, enter an additional comment or Take Action By date.
  5. Click Send.
