About Reports

You can create more than a dozen standard reports that quickly collect your company data into a printer-friendly format.

You generate reports from  > Employee Records > Reports.

The Reports page is organized into seven sections. The first six sections allow you to choose which employees will be included in the report. The reports in the Export Data section cover all of your employees and allow you to export that information to Microsoft Excel or other spreadsheet or database programs.

Three of the reports allow you to specify other parameters such as a date range:

  • Employment Report
  • Compensation Report
  • Activity Log Report
