Modifying an Invoice

You can modify the information in any invoice that has not been canceled  or submitted for approval. Invoices that have been canceled or submitted for approval must be reopened before any changes can be made.

Changes you can make include modifying any of the existing information, or adding and deleting invoice items.

To modify an invoice

  1. Open an invoice with a status of New or Open.
  2. Make any desired changes.
    • Enter any new or change information directly in the fields.
    • To add invoice items, click Add Item or Add 5 Items.
    • To delete an invoice item, click the Delete link next to the item.

    If you added or deleted invoice items or changed the quantity, rate, or tax status or changed the tax rate, the new invoice amount is calculated automatically.
  3. Click Save & Return.
