If an employee has lost a password or user ID, you can resend the e-mail notice on how to gain access to the system. For security, a new temporary password is sent.

To resend an employee the e-mail notice

  1. Click the Access tab.
  2. Click the Select box to identify the employee or employees.
  3. Click reset password to generate a new password and send the employee a new e-mail notice explaining how to gain access to the site.
