Changing Your Password

Passwords much be 8 to 16 characters long and contain no spaces. You must also know your current password to change to a new one.

To change your password

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Password.
  2. Enter your current password. For security, the characters are represented as asterisks (*).
  3. Enter your new password.
  4. Enter your new password a second time, for confirmation.
  5. Click Save.
