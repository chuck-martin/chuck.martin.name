Modifying a Pay Frequency

To modify a pay frequency

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Setup.
  2. Click Pay Frequency.
  3. Select the check boxes for any pay periods you want available, or clear the check boxes for any pay period you do not want available.
  4. For each pay frequency selected, make any desired changes to the date details.

    5. Pay Frequency

    Options

    Weekly

    Enter the date that will be the last day of your first pay week.

    Every Two Weeks

    Enter the date that will be the last day of the first two-week pay period.

    Twice a month

    Select the 2 dates each month that will be the last days of each pay period in the month. Pay is 24 times per year (twice per month).

    Monthly

    Enter a day of the month that will be the last day of the pay period.
  5. Click Save & Return.

Note: For InterPay users, once you define your original pay frequencies and the InterPay processing setup is complete (typically 7-12 days from the date this information is sent to InterPay), these fields become non-editable. If you need to make changes after this, contact InterPay directly.
