The Emergency Contacts Information page contains information about your emergency contacts. It may be seen and modified by you or an HR Administrator. In this page you can add or modify details for an individual emergency contact.

What do you want to do?

Identify this emergency contact as the primary emergency contact
Identify this emergency contact as also a dependent
Use your home address for this emergency contact
Delete this entire entry

To identify this emergency contact as the primary emergency contact

  1. Select the Primary Contact check box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To identify this emergency contact as also a dependent

  1. Select the Listed as Dependent check box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To use your home address for this emergency contact

  1. Select the Use home address as employee check box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To delete this entire entry

Click Delete.
