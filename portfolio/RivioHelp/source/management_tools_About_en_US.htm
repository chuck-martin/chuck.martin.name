About Management Tools

Management Tools is stuffed with best-practices forms that can assist you in all areas of your business. All the forms are downloadable and modifiable. All the forms are either Microsoft Word files (in RTF format) or Microsoft Excel worksheets.

You must have System Administrator or HR Administrator access rights to access these forms.

The forms are divided into the following categories:

  • Business Finance
  • Compensation & Benefits
  • Firing & Termination
  • Marketing
  • Recruiting & Hiring
  • Starting Your Business
  • Vehicles & Equipment
  • Worker Safety
