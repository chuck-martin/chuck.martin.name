About Expense Reports

Expense Reports contain standard items for completing and submitting expense reports. Although you can enter, track, submit, and approve expense reports completely online, because some companies require hard copies (printouts), you can also print the expense reports you submit.

Typically, the expense report process is like this:

  1. Add and fill out a new expense report.
  2. Submit the expense report to your manager.
  3. Your manager approves the expense report, sending it on to be paid.
  4. Payroll processes and pays the expense report.
  5. You get a check.
  6. Optionally, you can mark the expense report as complete.

Before you start using Expense Reports, you should set up your customers and projects in the Accounting area and set up expense report numbering.
