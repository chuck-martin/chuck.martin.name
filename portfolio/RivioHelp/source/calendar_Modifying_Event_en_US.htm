Modifying an Event
Modifying an event that happens once is slightly different from modifying a repeating event.

To modify an event

  1. Open the event by clicking on the event title.
  2. Make any desired changes.
  3. Click Save.

To modify a repeating event

Note: You can modify only all occurrences of the repeating event, not just one occurrence in the series.

  1. Open the event by clicking on the event title.
  2. Click on the button at the top that shows the date of the first occurrence of the event.
  3. Make any desired changes.
  4. Click Save.
