Use the Employment tab to record employment and termination information.

Employment information includes the employee's status, start date, and citizenship. Termination information includes the termination date, reason, and eligibility for rehire.

The Comment area lets you make notes that are only visible to others with HR Administrator privileges.

What do you want to do?

Enter or modify employment information or termination information
Enter information before an employee starts work
Transfer employee information to the Payroll system

To enter or modify employment information

  1. Click , then under Employee Records, click Employee Info.
  2. Click an employee name.
  3. Click Employment.
  4. Enter the information in the appropriate fields.
  5. Click Save & Return.

To enter information before an employee starts work

  1. Select an Employment Status of Pending.
  2. Enter the new employee's information.
  3. Click Save & Return.

To transfer employee information to the Payroll system

  1. Click Yes next to "Do you wish to use this employee in the Payroll Application?"
  2. Click Save & Return.
