Publishing a Company Announcement

Company announcements appear on Quick View pages, but they do not automatically get posted there when you add a new one. You must explicitly choose to display the announcement company-wide.

You must have System Administrator or HR Administrator access rights to publish a company announcement.

To publish a company announcement

  1. Click , then under Desktop View, click Quick View.
  2. In the Announcements area, click Go to Setup.
  3. Click the Display On Quick View check box of the announcement you want to publish.
  4. Click Save & Return.
