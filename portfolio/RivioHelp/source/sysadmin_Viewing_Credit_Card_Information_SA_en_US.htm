Viewing Credit Card Information

The Credit Card Info page shows information about the credit card you are using to pay for your monthly subscription and your per-user charges. (This is completely separate from any charges made in the Goods and Services area.)

You must have System Administrator access rights to view credit card information.

To view credit card information

  1. Click  , then under System Admin, click Account Info.
  2. Click Credit Card Info.
