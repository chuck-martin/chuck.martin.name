Modifying Procedures

You must have HR Administrator or System Administrator access rights to modify a procedure.

To modify a procedure

  1. Click  , then under Company Policies, click Setup.
  2. Click Procedures.
  3. Click the title of the procedure you want to modify.
  4. Enter your modifications. If you want to edit a step within the procedure, click the Edit link next to the step, make your modifications, then click Save & Return.
  5. Optionally, you can select the checkbox to notify employees of this procedure change by e-mail.
  6. Click Save & Return.
