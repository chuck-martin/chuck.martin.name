Translating the Access Rights Codes in the Access Rights List

When you assign access rights, you see both the full name of the user role and a description of what that role represents — what it can access and what it cannot. In the Access Rights List, these roles are abbreviated:

  • SA — System Administrator
  • HR — Human Resource Administrator
  • AA — Accounting Administrator
  • PA — Payroll Administrator
  • MG — Manager
  • EE — Employee
  • NA — No Access
