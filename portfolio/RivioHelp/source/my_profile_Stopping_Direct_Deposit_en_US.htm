Stopping Direct Deposit

Note: Stopping direct deposit removes all account information from the system. You will still need to contact your Payroll Administrator to stop your checks from being directly deposited.

To stop direct deposit

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click Direct Deposit.
  3. Select Do not use direct deposit.
  4. Click OK
  5. Click Save & Return.
