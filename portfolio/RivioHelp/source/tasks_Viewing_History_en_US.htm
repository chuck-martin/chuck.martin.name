Viewing Task History

All types of tasks record and retain a history of all actions, such as creation, approval, etc.

To view task history

  1. Open any type of existing taks or create a new task or any type.
  2. Click History & Notes.
    You can add an optional note at any time.
  3. If you want all participants in this task to be informed of the action you took (or the note you added), click Save & E-Mail Participants, otherwise, click Save & Return.
