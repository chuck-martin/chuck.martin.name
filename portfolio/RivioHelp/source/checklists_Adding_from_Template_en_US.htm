Adding a Checklist from a Template

To add a checklist from a template

  1. On the Quick View or the Task List page, from the drop-down list in the Task List section, select Checklist, then click Add.
    -or-
    On the Checklists page, click Add Checklist.
  2. On the Add Checklist page, click the name of the template you want to use.
  3. Change the title (required text) and description (optional text) to text that is meaningful for your specific task.
  4. Click Save.
