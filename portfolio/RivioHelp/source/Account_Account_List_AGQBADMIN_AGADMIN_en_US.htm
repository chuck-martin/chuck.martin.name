Using your Account List

The Account List shows you a list of accounts and sub-accounts (your Chart of Accounts). From here you can view, create, modify, and delete accounts and sub-accounts.

The Account List is available only if you have the QuickBooks Interface turned off. While you have the QuickBooks Interface on, you can import your Chart of Accounts from QuickBooks and use it anywhere you would use the accounts created from the Account List. When you turn the QuickBooks Interface off, you keep the imported chart of accounts. However, if you turn the QuickBooks Interface off and modify your accounts, those changes cannot be exported to your QuickBooks system.
