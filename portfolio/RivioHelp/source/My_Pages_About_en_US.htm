About My Pages

My Pages are three pages that show information snapshots, two Quick View pages and a personal Calendar page:

  • The My Work Quick View page (My Work > Desktop View > Quick View) provides links to common tasks and lists your current and past due tasks.
  • The Calendar page (My Work > Desktop View > Calendar) shows your appointments and other events.
  • The  Quick View page (  > Desktop View > Quick View) shows company announcements and lists the next two days' company events.
