Hiding Inactive Accounts

You can hide accounts that have a status of Inactive, making the list more manageable.

To hide inactive accounts

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Account List.
  2. Click Hide Inactive.
    Note: If inactive accounts are already hidden, the Hide Inactive button will be replaced with Show All.
