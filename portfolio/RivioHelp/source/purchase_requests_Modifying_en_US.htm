Modifying a Purchase Request

You can modify an open purchase request if you need to add, delete, or change the details of purchase items or change other items, such as the preferred vendor or date required.

To add a purchase request item

  1. Open the purchase request.
  2. In the New Items section, enter the information about each item description, quantity, and unit price.
  3. Click Save & Return.

To delete a purchase request item

  1. Open the purchase request.
  2. Click Delete next to the item you want to delete.
  3. Click Save & Return.

To change the details of a purchase request item

  1. Open the purchase request.
  2. In the Purchase Request Items section, change the desired information about any item.
  3. Click Save & Return.
