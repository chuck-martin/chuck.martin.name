Resetting a Forgotten Password

If you forget your password, it is possible to reset or re-establish it. For security reasons, you must have all of the following information:

  • Your company ID
  • Your user ID
  • Your e-mail address
  • Answer to your secret question

To reset or re-establish your password

  1. Go to the sign in page and click Forgot your password?
  2. Enter your Company ID, User ID, and E-Mail Address, then click Next.
    Note: You can continue only if you have entered your correct information.
  3. Enter your Secret Answer.
  4. Enter a new password in the Choose a New Password field.
  5. Re-enter your new password in the Confirm New Password field.
  6. Click Next.
  7. Click Sign In.

Confirmation e-mail notices will be sent to you and to your system administrator.
