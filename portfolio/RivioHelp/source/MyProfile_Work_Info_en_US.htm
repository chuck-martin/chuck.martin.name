Modifying Work Information

Use the Work tab of the Employee Information page to view information about your job or to change information that others can see about how to contact you at work.

You must be an HR Administrator to enter or change work information for any employee.

To enter or change work contact information

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Personal.
  2. Click Work.
  3. In the Work Contact Information section, enter the appropriate information.
  4. Click Save & Return.
