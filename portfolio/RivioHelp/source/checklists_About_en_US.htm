About Checklists

Checklists are tasks containing one or more items that can be checked off as you progress through the task. Checklists can be used to break down standard business processes and organize them into a series of individual tasks that take you step-by-step to completion of the business process.

Checklist features include:

  • Online preparation and routing of checklists
  • E-mail notification of checklist assignment and status changes
  • Checklist display, allowing easy management and status tracking of checklists in progress
  • History and Comments log, providing status and audit trail for action taken on checklists
  • Ability to customize checklists using more than 30 predefined templates or create one from scratch
  • Ability to reuse customized checklists for personal use or publish them for company-wide use
  • Access to more than 30 predefined checklist templates for Office Management, Human Resources, Legal, Marketing, Finance, and more

The table below explains several key characteristics of your checklists:

Characteristic Description
Type Shows the template used, if any (otherwise Checklist).
Title Shows the title of the checklist.
From Shows the user who transferred the checklist to you.
Assigned Date Shows the date that the checklist was created or transferred to you.
Status Shows the status of the checklist: New, Open, Canceled, or Done.

Checklist tasks can contain steps. Each step has a check box that you can check off as you complete that step. You can also create groups of steps when you find such groupings helpful.
