Viewing or Printing a Previously Created Payroll

You can view or print any previously created payrolls.

To view or print a previously created payroll

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Processing.
  2. In the Payroll Periods section, click the date range of a payroll
    -or-
    click Completed or Archived, then click the date range of a payroll.
  3. If the payroll status is not Completed, click Next on each screen until you get to the last page of the payroll.
  4. Click Print.

Note: If you use InterPay for your payroll processing, a payroll with a status of Completed cannot be resubmitted or changed.
