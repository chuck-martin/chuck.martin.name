Setting Access Rights

You must have System Administrator access rights to set access rights for other employees.

To set access rights

  1. Click  , then under System Admin, click Access Rights.
  2. Click Assign Access.
    This list is of employees whose information has been entered but have never before been assigned access rights, or of any other employees who currently have no access rights.
  3. Click a name.
  4. Select one or more levels of access rights.
  5. If there is no e-mail address, enter the employee's e-mail address.
  6. Click Create Account.

When you give employees access rights, an e-mail notice is sent containing your company ID, their new User ID, and a temporary password. The User ID and temporary password are sent in separate e-mail notices for security.
