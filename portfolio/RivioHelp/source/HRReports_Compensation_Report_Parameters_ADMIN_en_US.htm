The Pay Rate Report parameters page allows you to specify selection criteria. You may specify a salary that is any amount or is greater than or less than specified amounts. You may group the report by Department, FLSA Status, or Full-Time/Part-Time.

To set parameters and generate a Pay Rate report

  1. Fill in the appropriate salary range values in the appropriate fields, or click Any Amount.
  2. Select the grouping parameter to use.
  3. Click Generate Report.
