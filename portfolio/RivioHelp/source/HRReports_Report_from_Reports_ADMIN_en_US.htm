Running a Report from the Reports Page

When you run reports from the Reports page, you select the type of report first, and then select the employees you want included in the report.

  • Three of the reports allow you to specify other parameters such as a date range: Employment Report, Compensation Report, Activity Log Report.
  • Thirteen other reports are simple reports that only require you to select which employees are included: Employee Directory, Employee Profile, Employee Census, Work Phone List, Home Phone List, Emergency List, Birthday List, New Hire List, Termination List, Department List, Supervisor List, Anniversary List, Compensation History Report.
  • Six other options on the Reports page are not formatted reports but instead allow you to export data on all of your employees in a format that can be used in Microsoft Excel or other spreadsheets or database programs. The choices correspond to the tabs in the Employee Records form.

What do you want to do?

Generate a parameter-based Employment Report, Compensation Report, or Activity Log Report
Generate a simple report (not a parameter-based report)
Export data from the employee records

To generate a parameter-based Employment Report, Compensation Report, or Activity Log Report

  1. Click on one of the above report types.
  2. On the Select Employees page, select the employees you wish to be covered by the report.
  3. Click Next. The parameter form appears.
  4. Set the parameters for the report you are creating.
  5. Click Generate Report. The report appears in a new browser window.

To generate a simple report (not a parameter-based report)

  1. Click on the report type from the first five categories on the Reports page (not one of the above parameter-based reports nor an Export Data report).
  2. On the Select Employees page, select the employees you wish to be covered by the report.
  3. Click Generate Report. The report appears in a new browser window.

To export data from the employee records

  1. Click on the data type from the Export Data section. The choices correspond to the tabs in the Employee Records form. This will generate a file to be downloaded.
  2. Depending on your browser configuration, the file will be immediately opened in your spreadsheet program, or you will be offered the choice of opening it from its current location (on our system) or saving it as a file on your computer. Save the file.
  3. If your report opens in a spreadsheet, click File > Save As, and pick a location and filename for the new file. Click Save.
