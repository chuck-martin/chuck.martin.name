Canceling a Subscription

Only users with Rivio Administrator, CSR Supervisor, and CSR with Deactivation access privileges can cancel a subscription.

To cancel a subscription

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Company List.
  2. Find the company whose subscription you want to cancel by entering information about the company in the search fields and then clicking Find.
  3. Click the company name.
  4. Click User Information (only the company's System Administrator can request a subscription be cancelled) and verify the information:
    • Ask for the e-mail address.
    • Ask the Secret Question, enter the answer given, and click Check Answer. (make sure the answer is correct).
  5. Click Account Information and review the current subscription information.
  6. In the Acct. Status drop-down list box, select Inactive.
  7. Click Save.

A confirmation e-mail will be sent to the System Administrator.
