Entering Information About Your Company

During the registration process, you enter basic information about your company, such as the company name and address, contact information, and description. This information will appear in the Company Profile area, along with your company's fax numbers, and ZIP codes.

After registration, you should ensure this information is accurate and does not need to be updated. To do this, click , then under  Info, click Profile.

For more information, see Modifying the Company Profile.
