Adding a Partner's Information

In the Customer Service Console, partner information is the user information of the person who will have CSR Supervisor access rights for that partner.

Only users with Rivio Administrator access privileges can modify a partner's information.

To modify a partner's information

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Partner List.
  2. On the Partner tab, find the partner you want to modify and click on the partner name.
    • If the name is not already displayed on the list, you can type a larger number in the Show rows box and then click that link, or use the <<First, <Prev, Next> and Last>> links to scroll through the list.
    • You can also enter information about the partner in the search fields and click Search.
  3. Modify any desired information about the representative.
  4. Click Save.
