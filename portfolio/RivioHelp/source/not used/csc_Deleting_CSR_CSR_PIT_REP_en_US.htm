Deleting a Customer Service Representative

A Customer Service Representative (CSR) can have different types of access privileges and can perform customer service functions that depend on those access privileges.

Only users with Rivio Administrator, CSR Supervisor, or PIT Supervisor access privileges can delete a customer service representative.

To delete a customer service representative's data or access

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Representative List.
  2. On the Representatives tab, find the customer service representative you want to modify and click on the name.
    • If the name is not already displayed on the list, you can click a different page number or Show All to see more names.
    • You can also enter information about the customer service representative in the search fields and click Search.
  3. At the bottom of the page, click Delete.
  4. Click Save.

Note: You can also deactivate customer service representatives without deleting them by setting their privileges to None.
