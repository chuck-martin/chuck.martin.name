About Payment Info
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

Payment Info enables you to view and modify credit card information and billing addresses used for orders. You can also set a default credit card automatically used by all employees during checkout. Employees can set an alternate credit card during the shopping cart checkout process only if you have given them this option in Preferences.

You can open this page two ways.

To open Payment Info directly

 
  • Click  Payment Info on the left menu. This opens a list of company credit cards you can view and modify.

To open it from the Shopping Cart

 
  1. Click Shopping Cart.
  2. After shopping and selecting your items, click either the checkout button that includes the appropriate vendor's name (e.g. [Vendor Name] Checkout), or the Check Out All Vendors button.
  3. From the Checkout page, click the Change Card button under Credit Card Info (on the left). This opens a list of company cards you can view and modify.
