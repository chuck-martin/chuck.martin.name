Modifying Ship-To Info
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

If you are the purchasing administrator, or have been given authorization by the purchasing administrator, you can modify ship-to information. All authorized users can open the Ship-To Info page from the Checkout area. Purchasing administrators can also open it from the left menu.

After opening the Ship-To Info page from the Checkout area or left menu, you have several options for modifying your existing information. These include setting an address as a  default, deleting an address, and changing the ship-to address.

To set a default address

 
  1. Go to the Use This column.
  2. Mark the field in the appropriate row.

To delete an address

 
  1. Go to the Delete column.
  2. Click on the  X in the row of the address you want to delete.

To modify existing addresses

 
  1. Click the icon in the appropriate row under the Modify to open the Ship-To Address Detail page.
  2. Enter the new address.
  3. Click  OK.

To add new ship-to information

  1. Click the  Add New button to the right.
  2. Enter the appropriate information on the Ship-To-Address Detail page.
