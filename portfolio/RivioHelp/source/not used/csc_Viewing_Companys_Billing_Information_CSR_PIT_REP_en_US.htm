Viewing a Company's Billing Information

Only users with Rivio Administrator, CSR Administrator, and CSR with View Billing Info access privileges can view a company's billing information.

To view a company's billing information

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Company List.
  2. Find the company whose billing information you want to view by entering information about the company in the search fields and then clicking Find.
  3. Click the company name.
  4. Click Account Information.
  5. At the bottom of the page, click Click to View Billing and Payment History.
  6. Click a date to see the billing details for that billing period.
