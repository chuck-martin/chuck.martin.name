What is outsourced setup?

Outsourced setup is a service that  offers to help you get started on your  account as quickly as possible. Once you've registered to use  and elected to utilize the outsourced setup service, your personal setup assistant will contact you to determine your exact needs and will then set up your site according to your instructions.

How do I request outsourced setup?

You must first register for  before you can request outsourced setup. After you register, you will arrive on a Setup Options page. Select the first option, Outsource, and follow the instructions.

During outsourced setup, a temporary user account will be created for a representative of . Upon completion of outsourced setup, you will disable this temporary user account. You will not incur any fees for this temporary account.

By requesting outsourced setup, you authorize access to and use of your data by a representative of  solely for the purpose of setting up your account.

How secure is outsourced setup?

takes the protection of your information very seriously -- from the time you provide your company information to a setup assistant designated by  until the setup assistant finishes setting up your account. Any information provided to the setup assistant in paper form will be locked in a locked file cabinet inside a locked room that is accessible by only properly authorized setup assistants. If your data is contained within computer files, we take precautions to prevent the files from being copied or forwarded. No one, including your setup assistant, can view the password you have selected at any time. Nonetheless, we recommend changing your password periodically to insure the maximum level of security.

How do I disable my setup assistant?

To disable your setup assistant, click . In the System Admin area, click System Setup, then click the Disable Setup Assistant icon.

Note: You should disable the setup assistant only after you have been notified that outsourced setup of your account is complete and you have verified that your account has been set up according to your instructions. By disabling the setup assistant, you acknowledge that you have reviewed the manner in which your account has been set up and agree that it has been set up according to your instructions. If you disable the setup assistant before receiving notification that outsourced setup of your account is complete, you may interfere with successful completion of the setup process.
