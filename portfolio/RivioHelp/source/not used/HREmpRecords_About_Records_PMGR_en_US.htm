About Employee Records

The Employee Records area is where you manage the general and payroll information about your employees.

  • The Employee Info page contains a list of all employees. You can sort the list by any column by clicking the column header. You can add new employees and modify the information about existing employees.
  • The Payroll Info page contains a list of all the employees on your payroll.
  • The Reports page conations a list of pre-defined reports.
