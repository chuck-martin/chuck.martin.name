Viewing Additional Order Details
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

Clicking My Orders in the left menu displays the Order List, including order number, vendor, date the order was placed, and status. The purchasing administrator can also see the name of the user who placed the order.

To view additional details

  • Click the icon in the appropriate row under the View Order column. The resulting Order Detail page shows the name of user placing the order, ship-to and payment information, a breakdown of items ordered and costs. (Note: You can also open this page by clicking the appropriate order number from the Order List.)

For more information on items ordered

  • On the Order Detail page, click the item name under Description. This opens the Product Detail page, which includes an item description, price, and other information.
  • Click the Questions about this Order button to send an e-mail message to your company's purchasing administrator, who handles all correspondence with vendors.
