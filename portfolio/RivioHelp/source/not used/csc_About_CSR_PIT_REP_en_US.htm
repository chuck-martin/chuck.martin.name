About the Customer Service Console

The Customer Service Console provides functions to help customer service representatives find information about customers and assist them with their account and billing requests. Using the Customer Service Console, you can

  • Reset customer passwords
  • Make plan changes or escalate refund requests
  • Delete customer accounts
  • Escalate customer service issues
  • Set up and administer customer support representative access
