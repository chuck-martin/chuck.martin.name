About Ship-To Info
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

Ship-To Info enables you to view and modify ship-to addresses for your company. Users can make changes to ship-to information during the shopping cart checkout process only if you have given them this option in Preferences.

You can open this page two ways.

To open Ship-To Info directly

 
  • Click  Ship-To Info on the left menu. This opens a list of company addresses you can view and modify.

To open it from the Shopping Cart

 
  1. On the left menu, click  Shopping Cart.
  2. After shopping and selecting your items, either click the checkout button that includes the appropriate vendor's name (e.g. [Vendor Name] Checkout) or the Check Out All Vendors button.
  3. From the Checkout area, click the  Change Address button under Ship-To Address. This opens a list of company addresses you can view and modify.
