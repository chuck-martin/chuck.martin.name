About Shopping Carts
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

Products and services you select from item lists are placed in a shopping cart. The left menu shows the number and cost of items you have selected.

You have two ways to view your shopping cart

 
  • Click Shopping Cart on the left menu.
    -or-
  • Click the Return to Shopping Cart button (under Add) on the right side of any Search Results or Product Detail page.

Note: When you check out your order for a particular vendor, our system automatically sends the vendor your name, e-mail address, ship-to and payment details, and the list of items in your shopping cart. This enables the system to set up your account with the vendor and retrieve a preview of your order. Your credit card is not charged until you click the Final Submit button on the Checkout page.
