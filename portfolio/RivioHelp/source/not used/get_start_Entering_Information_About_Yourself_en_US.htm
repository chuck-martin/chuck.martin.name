Entering Information About Yourself

When your account was created, certain basic information was entered about you to establish an employee record for you. When you access the site for the first time, you should verify that the information contained in your Personal Profile is up-to-date and complete. Some information about you, such as your pay rate, can only be changed by the appropriate administrator.

When you sign in for the first time, you will see the Terms of Service. You must Accept the Terms of Service to gain access to the site. You are then asked to create a new Password and to select a secret question and enter the answer. You then have the option to Update My Profile or to Go to Quick View and start exploring the site.

If you choose Update My Profile, you are taken to your Personal Profile, where you can make entries and modifications to your employee record. If you choose Go to Quick View, you can navigate to your Personal Profile by clicking the My Work tab, then clicking My Profile and Personal in the left menu.

My Personal Profile is divided into several tabs. To update your profile click the corresponding tab, make your entries, and click Save. See also About My Personal Profile.
