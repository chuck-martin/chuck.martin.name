Deleting a Company

When you delete a company, all the user data for that company will be deleted from the database. (You should not delete a company if a request is being made to cancel a subscription.)

Only users with Rivio Administrator, CSR Supervisor, and CSR with Delete Account access privileges can delete a company.

To delete a company

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Company List.
  2. Find the company that you want to delete by entering information about the company in the search fields and then clicking Find.
  3. Click the company name.
  4. Click User Information (only the company's System Administrator can request that the company be deleted) and verify the information:
    • Ask for the e-mail address.
    • Ask the Secret Question, enter the answer given, and click Check Answer. (make sure the answer is correct).
  5. Click Account Information.
  6. In the Accout Status drop-down list box, select Delete Requested.
  7. Click Save.

A confirmation e-mail will be sent to the System Administrator.
