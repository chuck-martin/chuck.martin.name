Adding Items To Your Shopping Cart
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

After you have found products or services you need by using a search engine or browsing categories, you can add the items directly to your shopping cart from the Search Results page.

To add items to your shopping cart

  1. Enter the number of each selected item in the quantity (Qty.) field to the right.
  2. Click  Add to place the item(s) in your shopping cart. The new quantity and price is displayed under Shopping Cart on the left menu.
