Checking Out Your Shopping Cart
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

After making any necessary adjustments, you can check out items ordered from a particular vendor, or check out your entire order (from all vendors).

To check out items from one vendor

 
  • Click the [Vendor Name] Checkout button under the Subtotal column of the list of vendor's products selected. This displays the payment and ship-to information for this order, as well as the summary of the order, including total cost. Your credit card will not be charged until you click the Final Submit button from the order summary page.
 

To check out items from all vendors

 
  • Click the Checkout All Vendors button on the lower right side of the page. This displays the payment and ship-to information for all orders in your shopping cart, separated by vendor. It also includes a summary of the orders with total cost. Your credit card will not be charged until you click the Final Submit button.

Note: When you click Final Submit, the system sends the vendor your e-mail address, ship-to and payment details, and other information necessary to finalize the order.
