Viewing and Modifying Shopping Lists
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

You can view, add items, and make other changes to shopping lists before processing them.

To view a shopping list

 
  1. Click  Shopping Lists on the left menu.
  2. Under Modify, click the icon in the row of the list you want to view, or click the name of the list. This opens the Shopping List form.

You can make several changes from this form.

To add items to the list

 
  1. Enter the item in the Item Description field.
  2. Enter the Quantity.
  3. Click Add Item to List

If you have finished adding items to the list

 
  • Click the Process List at the bottom of the page to begin filling requests.

To delete the list

 
  • Click the Delete button, also at the bottom of the page.
