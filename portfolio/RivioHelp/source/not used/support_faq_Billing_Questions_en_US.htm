Does my subscription include the InterPay payroll processing service or Microsoft bCentral applications?

Your subscription does not include the InterPay payroll processing service or Microsoft bCentral applications; these must be subscribed to separately.

Can I change the credit card being charged?

To change the credit card being charged for your subscription, please contact your company administrator (the person who established your company account). If you are the company administrator, to change your credit card, click  > System Admin > Account Info. Then click Credit Card Info.

Top

When will my credit card be charged for my subscription and what do the charges represent?

If you enrolled in a monthly subscription plan, your credit card will be charged on the first calendar day of each month following the date of your subscription. The initial charge will be the prorated charge for the previous month, and applicable usage-based fees for the previous month, and the monthly base subscription fee for the coming month. All subsequent charges will consist of two types of fees: a base subscription fee for that month and, if applicable, usage-based fees for activity in the preceding month.

If you enrolled in an annual subscription plan, your credit card will be charged the annual base fee on the first calendar day of the month following your subscription date. If your plan charges usage-based fees, you will incur these fees on the first calendar day of each month following your subscription date; these fees are assessed based on activity in the preceding month.

If your specific subscription plan includes an up-front promotional period (for example, no charges for 30 days), your credit card will be charged when that promotional period ends, in accordance with the timing described above.

To view the specifics of your plan, click  > System Admin > Account Info. Then click Plan Informantion.

How do you count user accounts for the purposes of charging per-user fees?

If your subscription plan charges per-user fees, we assess these fees according to the number of active user accounts you had during the course of the calendar month as of the last day of that month. To view current active user accounts, click  > System Admin > Access Rights. Then click Notify Employee/Change Access Rights. (Only the System Administrator can view these user accounts.)

Guest accounts do not incur fees.

To view the specifics of your plan, click  > System Admin > Account Info. Then click Plan Information.

Top

How do you count employee records for the purposes of charging per-employee record fees?

If your subscription plan charges per-employee record fees, we assess these fees according to the number of employee records you had during the course of a calendar month as of the last day of that month. To view current employee records, click  > Employee Records > Employee Info. (Only the Human Resources or System Administrator can view all employee records.)

Employee records having a status of Terminated do not incur fees.

To view the specifics of your plan, click  > System Admin > Account Info. Then click Plan Information.
