Viewing and Deleting Orders
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

In My Orders, you can view or eliminate current or previously placed orders.

To view general order information

  • Click  My Orders in the left menu to display a list of orders, dates they were placed, and current status.

To delete an order

  • From the list of orders, go to the Delete column and click the  X in the row of the item you want to delete. This eliminates the item from the list.

You have two ways to view detailed order information

  • In the View Order column, click the icon in the row of the appropriate order.
    -or-
    Under Order Number, click the number of the appropriate item.

Both take you to a page with additional details on products and services ordered.
