Adding a Customer Service Representative

A Customer Service Representative (CSR) can have different types of access privileges and can perform customer service functions that depend on those access privileges.

Only users with Rivio Administrator or CSR Supervisor access privileges can add a customer service representative.

To add a customer service representative

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Representative List.
  2. Click Add Representative.
  3. Enter the information about the representative:
    Information type Description
    First Name,
    Last Name    		 The name of the customer service representative. Required fields.
    E-mail address The e-mail for the customer service representative. Sign in and other information will be sent to this address. Required field.
    Company ID The identifier of the partner company that the customer service representative works for. (If not a Rivio Administrator, this field is non-editable.)
    User ID Enter a User ID that the customer service representative will use to sign in. The User ID must be 3-16 characters long and have no spaces. Required field.
    Password,
    Confirm Password    		 Enter a password that the customer service representative will use to sign in. The password must be 8-16 characters long and contain no spaces. Required field.
  4. Select the privileges you want to assign to the customer service representative. (Required)
    If you're a Rivio Administrator, you can assign any privileges.
    If you're a CSR Supervisor, you can assign only CSR privileges.
  5. Click Save.
