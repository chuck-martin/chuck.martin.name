Adding a Partner

In the Customer Service Console, adding a partner means adding the user information of the person who will have CSR Supervisor access rights for that partner.

Only users with Rivio Administrator access privileges can add a partner.

To add a partner

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Partner List.
  2. Click Add Partner.
  3. Select a partner from the drop-down list.
  4. Enter the name and e-mail address of the person at the partner's company who will be assigned CSR Supervisor access rights.
  5. Select a Company ID.
  6. Enter a User ID and password.
  7. Click Save.
