Choosing Your Password

When your System Administrator activates an account for you, you receive two e-mail messages. The first e-mail contains the Company ID and User ID, the second e-mail has a new randomly generated password.

When you sign in for the first time you are asked to change your password, choose a secret question, and supply a secret answer. The secret question and answer provides a safety mechanism to allow you to change your password and access the site even if you have forgotten your password. Your original password is no longer valid.

Be sure to choose a password that you can easily remember and is 8-16 characters in length, with no spaces. It is good practice to use a combination of letters and numbers, avoiding full words or names that are easily discovered by others.

To gain access if you have forgotten your password, or wish to change your password, you will need four pieces of information:

  • Your Company ID
  • Your User ID
  • Your Work E-mail address
  • The answer to your secret question

You can change your password at any time.
