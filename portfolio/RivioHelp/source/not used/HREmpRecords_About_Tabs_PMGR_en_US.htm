About the Employee Information Tabs

The information in an individual Employee Record is divided into several tabs:

Tab

Contents

Personal

Use this to record the employee's personal information and personal contact information.

Work

This displays information about the employee's job and allows you to modify information that all employees can see about how to contact this employee at work.

Employment

This displays the employee's employment status.

Dependents

This gives you a list of the employee's dependents. From here you may modify the details about any dependent.

Emergency

This provides a list of the employee's emergency contacts. . From here you may modify the details about any Emergency Contact.
