What this Site Contains

This site contains a web application to help you manage your company payroll. To learn more about the features on this site and their benefits to your company, view our product sheets or start the Guided Tour.

Signing In

To enter the site you must have a Company ID, User ID, and password. Find out more about Signing In.

Navigating the Site

The menu on the left displays categories for features. To open a feature, click a category to open the feature list, and then click the feature.

To quickly view all features for the site, click Site Map. The Site Map is a listing of every tab and its features. Click on any link in the Site Map to access that feature.

To keep your data secure, click Sign Out when you have finished working in the site. This will end your work session and sign you out of the system.

Getting Help

If you need help while using any of the features on this site, click Help. A help page appears giving you information about the application or site page you are viewing, along with instructions and related topics. If you need additional assistance, click on the Customer Support tab in the Online Help window. Find out more about Using Online Help.

Setting Up Your System

If you are an Administrator, you may wish to read the System Setup and Configuration manual. This manual contains useful information about the various administrative areas of the site, and how to get up-and-running. You will need Adobe Acrobat Reader to view this manual.
