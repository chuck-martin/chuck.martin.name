The Process List page provides a starting point for finding office supplies requested through a shopping list.

To shop for an item on the list

 
  1. Go to the row of the item.
  2. Click the  Go Find button. This opens a Search Results page that lists items available and allows you to add items to your shopping cart.
  3. After you have found your items, select the ones you want to add to your shopping cart, and continue with checkout.

 Note: All items on this list will continue to appear on the shopping list (with quantity listed as 0) for future use.
