Modifying a Customer Service Representative's Data and Access

A Customer Service Representative can have different types of access privileges and can perform customer service functions that depend on those access privileges.

Only users with Rivio Administrator or CSR Supervisor access privileges can modify a customer service representative's data or access.

To modify a customer service representative's data or access

  1. Click the Customer Service Console tab, then in CSC, click Representative List.
  2. On the Representatives tab, find the customer service representative you want to modify and click on the name.
    • If the name is not already displayed on the list, you can click a different page number or Show All to see more names.
    • You can also enter information about the customer service representative in the search fields and click Search.
  3. Modify any desired the information about the representative:
    Information type Description
    First Name,
    Last Name    		 The name of the customer service representative. Required fields.
    E-mail address The e-mail for the customer service representative. Sign in and other information will be sent to this address. Required field.
    Company ID The identifier of the partner company that the customer service representative works for. (If not a Rivio Administrator, this field is non-editable.
    User ID Enter a User ID that the customer service representative will use to sign in. The User ID must be 3-16 characters long and have no spaces.
    Password,
    Confirm Password    		 Once a CSR account has been created, this field is non-editable because a password cannot be changed, only reset.
  4. Make any desired changes to the privileges you want to assign to the customer service representative. (Required field)
    If you're a Rivio Administrator, you can assign any privileges.
    If you're a CSR Supervisor, you can assign only CSR privileges.
  5. Click Save.
