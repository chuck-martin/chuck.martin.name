Creating Shopping Lists
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

With Shopping Lists, you can consolidate your company's orders into one list, and fill requests for products and services efficiently.

To create a shopping list

 
  1. Click  Shopping Lists on the left menu.
  2. Click the  Create New List button at right.
  3. Enter the appropriate information the fields at the top of the form. The list name and type are required.
  4. Use the  Due Date drop-down list to select the month, date, and year items are needed.
  5. In the  Item Description field to the left, enter the name of the item needed, then enter the quantity.
  6. Click  Add Item to List.

After adding items, you have two options

 
  • To save the list, click  Save & Return. This places your list with any others on the Shopping List main page.
    -or-
    To process your list, click  Process. You (or the person assigned the list) can begin filling the requests.
