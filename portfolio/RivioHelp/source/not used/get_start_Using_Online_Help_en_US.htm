Using Online Help

To get help for the page you are viewing, click the Help link at the top of the site. The online help window will appear displaying the relevant help page. You can easily access related topics by clicking the links within the help page.

If you need help on other activities you can navigate through the other books and topics displayed in the Contents tab. Click a book to open it, then click a topic to view the help page.

To browse through a list of Frequently Asked Questions click the Customer Support tab. Then click the Frequently Asked Questions book. Each topic of this book represents a category of questions. Click a topic to open the help page for that category.

To get help from a vendor or supplier on this site, open the Contact Information book, and click Contact Vendors. A list of vendors and how to contact them appears. To contact us with a customer support question, click Contact Us.
