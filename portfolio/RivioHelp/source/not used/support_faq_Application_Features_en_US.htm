What is Work Flow Manager™ messaging? How is it different from the Checklist Manager and Task Manager?

Rivio has created Work Flow Manager, a communication tool that is similar to e-mail in that it enables users to send and receive messages via the Internet. However, Work Flow Manager messaging differs from regular e-mail in that business logic has been built in to allow workflow, tracking, archiving, and history. Work Flow Manager messaging enables communication between Rivio's applications. Work Flow Manager acts as a workflow vehicle for the Task Manager and Checklist Manager applications. For example, when you send a Time Sheet or electronic form to someone in your company, Work Flow Manager is the communication tool that delivers and tracks the task.

What do the red asterisks (*) that are located next to some of the fields in the application forms mean?

A red asterisk (*) indicates that the field is required. Data must be entered into a required field before continuing a process or saving a form.

What is the difference between paper forms and electronic forms?

Paper forms are forms that you can download to your computer and print. These forms are in .RTF (rich-text format) and can be read by most word processing applications. Forms are provided in the following business areas:

  • Accounting
  • Human Resources
  • Legal
  • Purchasing
  • Sales
  • Shipping

You can find paper forms in the  tab under Forms and Tools. Electronic forms are the online forms that are part of the application features of this site. Electronic forms include:

  • Expense Reports
  • Invoices
  • Time-Off Requests
  • Purchase Requisitions
  • Time Sheets
