After my employees register, can they later establish their own passwords? How?

Yes. After they sign in with the randomly generated password sent to them in their Welcome e-mail, they will be required to change their password. They can always change their password any time after that by clicking the Password link under My Profile.

What do I do if I'm an employee and I forget my User ID or Password?

You can obtain a new password in one of two ways:

  1. On the Sign In page, click on the Forgot your password? link. You will need your Company ID, User ID, e-mail address, and Secret Answer. You will receive a confirmation e-mail.
  2. Click My Work > My Profile > Password, then enter your old and new password and click Save.
Top

What do I do if I'm an administrator and I forget my User ID or Password?

If you forget your password, click on the Forgot your password? link on the main Sign In page. You will receive a confirmation e-mail.
