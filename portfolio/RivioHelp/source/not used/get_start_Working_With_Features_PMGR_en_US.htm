Working With Features

This page gives you an overview of routine activities while working with features.

Viewing and Sorting Lists

Lists automatically display up to 10 records at a time by default. You can navigate through additional records by clicking a page number after Go To Page. As you pass your mouse over the page number, you'll see the index range of records contained on that page. To show all records in the list, click Show All.

Click a column header to sort any list. An arrow points up or down to indicate whether your list is sorted in ascending or descending order.

Finding Records in a List

You can quickly search for records in a list.

To find a record in a list

  • Click Open Search Box.
  • Enter the information for your search.
  • Click Search.

A list of records that fit your search criteria appears in the list.

Session Time Out Notice

For security purposes, you are signed out of the site after an extended period of no activity. You will receive a message two minutes prior asking if you wish to continue working. If you do not respond within the two minute period, you will automatically be signed out. To sign back in, return to the sign-in page.
