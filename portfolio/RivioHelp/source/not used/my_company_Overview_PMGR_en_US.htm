My Company Overview

Under the  tab, you can find information about your company. The different areas of the left menu each contain different types of information, such as company locations, employee records, and more.

Area

Contents

Profile

General information about the company, such as the headquarters address, phone number, e-mail addresses, Web site, and other company locations.

Employee Directory

Listing of all employees and basic information (such as phone and e-mail) of each.

Departments

Listing of departments within the company.
