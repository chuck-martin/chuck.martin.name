Adding New Payment Info
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

You can add new payment information on the Payment Detail page.

To open the Payment Detail page

 
  1. Click  Payment Info on the left menu.
  2. Click  Add New .

To add new credit card and billing information

 
  1. Enter the appropriate information in each field of the Payment Detail page. (An asterisk indicates a required field.)
  2. Click  OK .
