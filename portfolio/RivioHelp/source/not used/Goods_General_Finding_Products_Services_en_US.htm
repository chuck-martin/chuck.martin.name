Goods and Services offers four ways to shop for products and services:

  • Searching by keyword within a category
  • Searching by keyword within a particular  vendor listing
  • Browsing general categories
  • Browsing vendor categories
 

To search within a category

  1. Locate the Product Search field on the left menu, or click the Search for Products icon on the Goods and Services main page.
  2. Enter the desired product or service in the appropriate field.
  3. Using the drop-down list under the search field, select the category that best fits the product or service.
  4. Click Go next to the search field to display a list of products or services offered by competing vendors, with prices and other information.
 

To search within vendor listings

  1. Click Vendors on the left menu.
  2. Click the name (not the logo) of the desired vendor. The vendor search field appears on the left menu (below the Product Search field).
  3. Enter the item in the field and click Go to display a list of products or services offered by the vendor.
 

To find products and services by browsing general categories

  1. Click Categories in the left menu, or click Browse Categories on the Goods and Services main page.
  2. Find the appropriate category for your product and service.
  3. Locate your item under it.
  4. Click the item for a list of products or services.
 

To browse vendor categories

  1. Click Vendor in the left menu.
  2. Click the name of the desired vendor to retrieve the vendor's products or services by category.
  3. Find the item you need and click it for a list of related products or services offered by the vendor.
