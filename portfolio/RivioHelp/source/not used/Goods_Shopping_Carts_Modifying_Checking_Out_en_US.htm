Modifying and Checking Out Your Shopping Cart
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

Products and services you select from item lists are placed in a shopping cart. The left menu displays the number and cost of items you have selected. You can view your shopping cart by clicking Shopping Cart on the left menu.

Modifying

Your shopping cart offers several options for modifying its contents. You can:
  • Delete items by clicking the  X to the left of the product or service name.
  • Change the number of products ordered in the quantity field next to the product subtotal (on the right side of the page).
  • If the vendor has not set a shipping method, select one using the drop-down list under the List Price column for all products.

Checking Out

After making any necessary adjustments, you can check out items ordered from a particular vendor, or check out out your entire order (from all vendors).

To check out items from one vendor

 
  • Click the  [Vendor Name] Checkout button on the under the Subtotal column of the list of vendor's products selected.

To check out items from all vendors

 
  • Click the  Check Out All Vendors button on the lower right side of the page.
Both buttons open the Checkout page with buyer and payment information, and details on items ordered.

To complete the order

 
  • Click Final Submit from this order summary page. Your credit card will be charged at this time.

Note: When you click Final Submit, the system sends the vendor your e-mail address, ship-to and payment details, and other information necessary to finalize the order.
