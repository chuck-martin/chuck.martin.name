Technical Questions?

Here's how to contact Customer Support:

  • Submit a Customer Support Request online
  • E-mail customercare@rivio.com

Customer Support hours are from 6 a.m. to 6 p.m. (PT) Monday through Friday, except holidays.

When you contact our Support Team, please have the following information ready:

  • Your contact information
  • Operating system (Windows 95, 98, 2000, or NT)
  • Internet access method (modem or network connection)
  • Modem speed
  • Internet Service Provider name
  • Browser type and version
  • Description of the problem you are experiencing, including any error messages that appear
