Where to Start

After you have completed your Personal Profile, you're ready to get started using the site. Depending on your routines and company priorities, you may find the following features of particular help in managing your work.

My Work

The My Work tab includes features that help you manage workflow tasks; submit requests; update your payroll information for withholding, direct deposit, and deductions; and maintain your personal profile and preferences. The Quick View page in the My Work tab under Desktop View, lets you quickly glance through your pending and past due tasks.

Expense Reports

Expense Reports enables employees and managers to create, approve, submit, view, track, and archive expense reports. Employees can assign expenses to specific accounts and customers or projects for accounting and invoicing purposes. Expense report status and history are also easily viewable. Managers are notified by e-mail when their action is required in the process.

Typically, the expense report process involves these steps:

  1. Add and fill out a new expense report.
  2. Submit the expense report to your manager. If your company requires a hard copy of the expense report, you can print it first and then attach any required receipts
  3. Your manager approves the expense report, sending it on to be paid.
  4. The Payroll or Finance department processes and pays the expense report.
  5. You receive reimbursement.
  6. Optionally, you can mark the expense report as complete.

Click My Work, then click To Do, then click Expense Reports. Find out more about Expense Reports.

Your company can also publish policies and procedures for expense reports directly on the site. Policies are located under the My Company tab.

Time Sheets

Time Sheets enables employees to complete their time sheets and submit them for approval online. Employees can track their time by customer and project codes for accounting and invoicing purposes. Time sheet status and history are also easily viewable.

Administrators can specify time-off categories. Management reports allow for easy extraction of time sheet data for offline processes. Printable time sheet data and client/project time summary provides flexibility for use in third party applications for billing and payroll processing purposes.

Typically, the time sheets process involves these steps:

  1. Add and fill out a new time sheet.
  2. Submit the time sheet to your manager.
  3. Your manager approves the time sheet, then sends it on for processing.

Click My Work, then click To Do, then click Time Sheets. Find out more about Time Sheets.

Purchase Requests

Purchase Requests, enables employees to create and route purchase requests to managers for approval, or directly to individuals responsible for making purchases. Requests are easily tracked and managed with online routing, e-mail notification, and an audit trail

Typically, the purchase request process involves these steps:

  1. Add and fill out a new purchase request.
  2. Submit the purchase request to appropriate staff for approval and processing.
  3. You can view the status of your request in your list of purchase requests.

Click My Work, then click To Do, then click Purchase Requests. Find out more about Purchase Requests.

Your company can also publish policies and procedures for purchase requests directly on the site. Policies are located under the My Company tab.

Policies and Procedures

Administrators can easily publish policies and procedures for employees to reference online. Administrators have the option to:

  • Upload documents (such as an employee handbook) for employee viewing
  • Copy and paste text into the application that explains certain procedures or policies
  • Automatically notify employees via e-mail of new or changed documents

Publishing policies and procedures online makes it easy for employees to complete tasks and workflow activities according to company guidelines, without leaving the site.

Click My Company, then click Company Policies, then click either Policies or Procedures. Find out more about Policies and Procedures.
