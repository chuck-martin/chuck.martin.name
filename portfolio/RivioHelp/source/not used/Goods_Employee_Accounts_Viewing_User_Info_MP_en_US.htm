The Employee Accounts page provides a list of accounts, and the user IDs and passwords needed to gain access to them.

To open the Employee Accounts page

  1. Click Employee Accounts on the left menu.
  2. Click the name of the appropriate user from the list.

To modify the information in the Employee Accounts page

  • Find the row with the appropriate user and vendor, and click  Change. This opens a page where you can modify a user's ID and password.
