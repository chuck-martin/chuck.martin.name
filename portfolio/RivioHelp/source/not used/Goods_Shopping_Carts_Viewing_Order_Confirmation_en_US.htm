Viewing Your Order Confirmation
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

Your order confirmation appears after you click the Final Submit button from the order summary page. It includes your order number (if available) from the vendor and a reference number. The vendor also sends you the order number and other order information by e-mail.

You may receive a message that certain items are not available, or your order may fail for some reason (e.g. problems with the credit card information).

To try to resubmit a failed order

 
  • Click  Retry Orders.

To look for other products and services

  • Click  Continue Shopping.

To check out orders from other vendors

  • Click  Return to Shopping Cart.

To view the status of all orders

  • Click the  View Order List button below the confirmation. This takes you to My Orders.
