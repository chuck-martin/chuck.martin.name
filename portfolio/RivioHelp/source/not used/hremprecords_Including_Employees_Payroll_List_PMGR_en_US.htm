Including Employees in the Payroll List

Payroll records are a list separate from the main employee list, and inclusion on the employee list does not automatically mean inclusion on the payroll list. When you enter a new employee, you must specifically speicfy their placement on the payroll record list.

To include an employee in the payroll list

  1. Click  , then under Employee Records, click Employee Info.
  2. Click the name of an employee.
  3. Click Employment.
  4. Click Yes next to "Do you wish to use this employee in the Payroll Application?".
  5. Click Save.
