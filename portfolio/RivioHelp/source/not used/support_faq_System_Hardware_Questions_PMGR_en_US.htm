How do I find out what browser version I am using?

To find your browser version:

  1. Open your browser.
  2. From the Help menu, select About browser name. A window appears containing information about your browser. The number that appears next to your browser name is the version number.

How do I search for a specific record?

You can find records by clicking the Search button. A search criteria box will appear. Simply enter the search criteria for the record(s) you are looking for and click the Search button.

How do I find my Internet connection speed?

To find your Internet connection speed:

  1. From the Windows Start menu select Settings -> Control Panel.
  2. In the Control Panel window, double-click Modems to open the Modems Properties dialog box.
  3. The brand and model information for your modem appears in the dialog box. For modem property details including your Internet connection speed, click Properties.

What are the system requirements for using the site and its features?

Rivio recommends the following configuration:

  • An IBM compatible PC running Windows 95, Windows 98, or Windows NT 4.0 or later.
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer version 5.01 or newer, or Netscape Navigator 6.1 or newer. For optimal performance, you may wish to upgrade to the latest browser version.
  • A modem (or other Internet connection) capable of speeds of 28.8kbps or greater.
  • An Internet connection.

Other operating systems and browsers may work, as long as they support the required functionality. The browser must support JavaScript. Rivio is testing compatibility issues and is continually refining the user interface to eliminate the use of functions that are not supported by the most common browsers and operating systems.

How do I find out my screen resolution?

To find your screen resolution:

  1. From the Windows Start menu select Settings -> Control Panel.
  2. In the Control Panel window, double-click Display to open the Display Properties dialog box.
  3. Click the Settings tab to open the Settings page.
  4. Depending on the version of Microsoft Windows that you are running, the screen resolution will appear either in the Desktop Area box, Screen Area box, or next to the Display description. The screen resolution is described in pixels and appears in a format of width by height, for example 1024 x 768.

Why does the system send me back to the sign-in page?

The site is programmed to sign you out after a designated period of inactivity. This is done to ensure the security of your data and to prevent tampering by unauthorized users. You will receive a mesage 2 minutes prior asking if you wish to continue working. If you do not respond within the 2-minute period, you will automatically be signed out. To sign back in, return to the sign-in page.
