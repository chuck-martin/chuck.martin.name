Modifying Payment Info
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

If you are the purchasing administrator, or have been given authorization by the purchasing administrator, you can modify payment information. All authorized users can open the Payment Info page from the Checkout area. Purchasing administrators can also open it from the left menu.

After opening the Payment Info page, you have several options for modifying the existing information. These include setting a card as a default, deleting a card, and changing credit card and billing information.

 To set a credit card as a default payment method

  1. Go to the Use This column.
  2. Select the field in the appropriate row.

To delete a credit card from the list

  1. Go to the Delete column.
  2. Click the  X in the row of the card you want to delete.

To modify existing payment information

  1. Click the icon in the appropriate row under the Modify heading to open the Payment Detail page.
  2. Enter your credit card and billing information in the designated fields.
  3. Click  OK .

To add new payment info

  1. Click the Add New button to the right.
  2. Enter the appropriate information on the Payment Detail page.
