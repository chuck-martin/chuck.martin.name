Adding New Ship-To Info
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

You can add new ship-to information on the Ship-To Address Detail page.

To open it

 
  1. Click  Ship-To Info on the left menu.
  2. Click  Add New.

To add a new ship-to address

 
  1. Enter the appropriate information in each field of the Ship-To Address Detail page. (An asterisk indicates a required field.)
  2. Click  OK.
