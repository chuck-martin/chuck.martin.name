Modifying a Product or Plan

Changing product typically includes changing plan because different products can have different plans.

Only users with Rivio Administrator, CSR Administrator, and CSR access privileges can modify a company's partner or plan.

To change a partner or plan

  1. Click Customer Service Console, then in CSC, click Company List.
  2. Find the company whose product or plan you want to change by entering information about the company in the search fields and then clicking Find.
  3. Click the company name.
  4. Click Account Information.
  5. Click Edit.
  6. Select a product, then click Next.
    The company's current product is selected by default. If you are changing only the plan, do not make any other selection here.
  7. Select a plan, then click Save & Return.
