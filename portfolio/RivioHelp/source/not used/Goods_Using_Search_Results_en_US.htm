Using Search Results
Comment this out or delete above here before Check-In -->

After you have found the products or services you need by using a search field or browsing categories, you can:

 
  • Find detailed descriptions of the items.
  • Add your selections directly to your shopping cart from the Search Results page.

To find details on items

 
  • Click the item name. This opens a page with a detailed product description. You can also add the item to your shopping cart from here.

To add items to your shopping cart from Search Results

 
  1. Enter the number of each selected item in the quantity (Qty. ) field on the right side of the page.
  2. Click  Add to place the item(s) in your shopping cart. The new quantity and price is displayed on the left menu.
