About Employee Records

The Employee Records area contains a list of all of your employees. The list shows each employee's name, department, phone number and status. You can click on the employee name to get more detailed information.

You must have System Administrator access rights to view the detailed records. Users with Payroll Administrator access rights may view the records on the Payroll Info page only. Users with HR Administrator access rights may view the records on the Employee Info page only.
