About QuickBooks Integration

The Accounting area operates in two modes: with QuickBooks® integration on or off.

When QuickBooks integration is off (disabled), you have direct access to your Accounts list, but you cannot import or export account data from your QuickBooks files.

When QuickBooks integration is on (enabled), you can import and export account information from your QuickBooks files, but you no longer have direct access to the Accounts list. The information you can import and export includes your account list, customer list, employee list, and credit terms.

Note: If you turn QuickBooks integration off and modify your accounts, those changes cannot be exported to your QuickBooks system.
