About Purchase Requests

In Purchase Requests, you can create and route purchase requests to managers for approval, or directly to individuals responsible for making purchases. Features of the Purchase Requests area include:

  • Ability to specify item description, quantity needed, references to companies and projects, date items are needed, preferred vendor, and maximum purchase price per item.
  • Online preparation and routing of purchase requests.
  • Flexible routing capabilities, allowing you to easily follow company specific procedures for purchase requests.
  • E-mail notification of purchase request submission and status changes.
  • Convenient Purchase Request display, allowing for easy management and status tracking of outstanding requests.
  • E-mail notification when preset due dates are missed.
  • History log audit trail, helping to ensure proper company purchase request approval process has been followed.
