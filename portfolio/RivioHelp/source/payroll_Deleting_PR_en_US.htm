Deleting a Payroll

To delete a payroll from the Current page

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Processing.
  2. In the Payroll Periods section, select the check box next to a payroll.
  3. Click Delete.
  4. Click OK.

To delete a payroll from the Archived page

You cannot delete payrolls that are completed.

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Processing.
  2. Click Archived.
  3. Click Delete next to the payroll you want to delete.
  4. Click OK.
