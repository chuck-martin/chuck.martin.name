Setting Up Outsourcing

When you first sign up, and after you have entered the initial information about your company, you can choose to have a third party set up information about your employees, customers, accounts, payroll, and so on (outsourcing).

Outsourcing is an option available to you when you are first registering your company (after you have accepted the Terms of Service) and at other times through the System Setup page.

To set up outsourcing

  1. On the Outsource Setup page, enter your contact information and select the areas where you want setup outsourced.
  2. Click Save & Return.

A representative of the company that will input your company's information will contact you to obtain the necessary information. Once the data input is completed, you will receive e-mail stating that the outsourcing process is complete. You can then sign in and make sure all the information about your company is accurate.
