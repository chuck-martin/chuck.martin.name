Use the Work tab to record the employee's work information and work contact information.

Work information includes the employee's department, job title, and whom they report to. This form also includes contact information such as work address and work e-mail address.

The Comment area lets you make notes that are only visible to others with HR Administrator privileges.

To enter or modify Work Information or Work Contact Information

Enter the information in the appropriate fields.

To add a new department to the Department Name drop-down list

  1. Click Add Department.
    You can also reach the same page by clicking , then under , clicking Departments.
  2. Enter the appropriate information about the new department.
  3. Click Save & Return.
