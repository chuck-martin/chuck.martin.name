Deleting a Direct deposit Account

When you delete a direct deposit account, the information is not saved anywhere. If you want to add the same account at a later time, you will have to re-enter the information about the account.

To delete a direct deposit account

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click Direct Deposit.
  3. Click Delete for the account you want to delete.
  4. Click Save & Return.
