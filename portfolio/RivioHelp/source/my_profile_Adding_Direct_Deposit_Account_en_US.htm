Adding a Direct Deposit Account

You can store the information for up to 3 accounts.

To add a direct deposit account

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click Direct Deposit.
  3. Click Add.
  4. Enter the bank and account information and the amount you want to deposit.
    Field Information

    Bank Name

    (Required) The name of your bank (should match the official bank name).

    Account Type

    (Required) Select from Checking Account, Savings Account, or No direct Deposit.

    Bank City

    The city of your bank's main branch.

    Bank State

    The state of your bank's main branch.

    Bank Routing (ABA)

    (Required) The routing number on the bottom of your check (typically the first 9 numerals; click Sample Check to see an example).

    Bank Account No.

    (Required) Your account number.

    Dollar Amount

    The amount you want automatically deposited each pay period. Select Remainder of entire paycheck (a) if you have just one account entered, deposit all of your pay, or (b) if you have multiple accounts entered, deposit the amount remaining after the amounts in the other account(s) are deposited. This option can be selected for just one account.
  5. Click Save & Return.
