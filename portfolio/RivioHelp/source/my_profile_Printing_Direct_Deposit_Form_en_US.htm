Printing a Direct Deposit Form

You can easily print a form that contains all your current direct deposit information along with a space for a blank check.

Note: The direct deposit form can be printed only when direct deposit accounts are in use.

To print a direct deposit form

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click Direct Deposit.
  3. Click Printer Friendly Format.
  4. Click Print Page
