Setting Up Pay Frequencies

You can have up to 4 different pay frequencies: weekly, every two weeks, twice a month, and monthly. You can have more than one pay frequency active, as you may have different people who get paid on different schedules. The pay frequencies you set up here are what will be available for you to select when you create a payroll and in the HR employee record.

To set up pay frequencies

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Setup.
  2. Click Pay Frequency.
  3. In the Pay Frequency section, select the check boxes for any pay periods you want available.
  4. For each pay frequency you select, enter the date details, which are used to calculate the actual pay periods

    Pay Frequency

    Options

    Weekly

    Enter the date that will be the last day of your first pay week. Employees are paid 52 times per year.

    Every Two Weeks

    Enter the date that will be the last day of the first two-week pay period. Employees are paid 26 times per year (every 2 weeks).

    Twice a Month

    Select the 2 dates each month that will be the last days of each pay period in the month. Employees are paid 24 times per year (twice per month).

    Monthly

    Enter a day of the month that will be the last day of the pay period. Employees are paid 12 times per year.
  5. Click Save & Return.

Note: For InterPay users, once you define your original pay frequencies and the InterPay processing setup is complete (typically 7-12 days from the date this information is sent to InterPay), these fields become non-editable. If you need to make changes after this, contact InterPay directly.
