Modifying W-4

The W-4 tab on the Payroll Information page shows your personal W-4 information. This is the information that was used for your last pay period.

You can modify you filing status and the allowances for federal and state (if applicable) withholding.

Note: If you're not sure what to enter for your allowances, check the worksheets for W-4s, whcih you can get from your HR representative or the IRS web site.

To modify your W-4 information

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click W-4.
  3. Make the changes to the information.
  4. Click Save & Return.
    This will save your change on your Payroll Administrator's task list. You must also fill out a paper W-4 form because the federal government requires signed paperwork to process this change. The changes will not become permanent in the payroll system until your Payroll Administrator approves them.
