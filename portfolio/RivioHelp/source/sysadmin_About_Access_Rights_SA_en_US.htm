About Access Rights

Access rights control which parts of the system are available to users and are controlled using roles. You can assign more than one role to an individual user. A user's roles determine which major items in the left menu the user can open.

By default, when employee records are created, they do not have access at all. You must specify access rights. Most employees should get Employee (abbreviated EE) access rights.

Be cautious when granting System Administrator access (abbreviated SA). System Administrator is the "super" role. Anyone with System Administrator access can grant additional access rights to any employee and has access to your entire company's records.
