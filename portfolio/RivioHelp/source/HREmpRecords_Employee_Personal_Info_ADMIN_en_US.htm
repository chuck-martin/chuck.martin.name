Use the Personal tab to record the employee's personal information and personal contact information.

Personal information includes the employee's name, Social Security number, Employee ID, and gender. This form also includes contact information such as home address and pager number.

The Comment area lets you make notes that are only visible to others with HR Administrator privileges.

To enter or modify Personal Information or Personal Contact Information

  1. Click , then under Employee Records, click Employee Info.
  2. Click an employee name.
  3. Click Persnal.
  4. Enter the information in the appropriate fields.
  5. Click Save & Return.
