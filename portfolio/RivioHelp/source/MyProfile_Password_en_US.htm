Modifying My Password

The My Password Information page lets you change your system access password. You must use your current password to do so.

Your password was originally given to you either directly by your System Administrator, or in a pair of e-mail messages that were sent to you when your System Administrator set up your access to the system. When you sign in the first time, you must change your password. Your original password is no longer valid.

If you happen to forget your password, during the sign-in process you can use the secret question and secret answer specified here to verify your identity and request a new password.

What do you want to do?

Set a new password
Specify a "secret question" and "secret answer" in case you forget your password
Change your User ID

To set a new password

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Password.
  2. Enter your current password. For security, passwords are not displayed in readable form.
  3. Enter your new password.
  4. Enter your new password a second time, for confirmation.
  5. Click Save.

To specify a secret question and secret answer in case you forget your password

  1. Enter a question.
  2. Enter the answer you will give when asked the above question.
  3. Click Save.

To change your User ID

Only your System Administrator can change your User ID.
