Submitting a Time Sheet for Approval

To submit a time sheet for approval

  1. Click My Work, then under To Do, click Time Sheets.
  2. Click My Time Sheets.
  3. Click a time sheet with a status of Open or New.
  4. Click Submit for Approval.
  5. From the Select Name drop-down list, select a user
    -or-
    In the E-Mail Address field, type a valid e-mail address.
    Note: Only employees who have been assigned Manager access rights appear in the drop-down list.
  6. If desired, enter an additional comment or a date to take action by.
  7. Click Send.
