Sending an Invoice to a Client

You can send invoices by e-mail to clients. The clients receive guest accounts where they can route, approve, and submit invoices for payment within their own companies.

To send an invoice to a client

  1. If it is not already entered into Invoices, add the invoice.
  2. If your company requires you to do so, submit the invoice for approval (for example, to your controller).
  3. On the Invoice page, click Forward.
  4. Type the e-mail address of the person you want to send the invoice to.
  5. Click Send.
