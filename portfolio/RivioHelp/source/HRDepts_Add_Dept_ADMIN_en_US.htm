Use Add Department to create a new department name and ID. Go to this form directly from the Human Resources Department Directory. In other parts of the system, such as in Employee Records or when moving an employee to a different department, a choice of New takes you to this form to create a new department name.

What do you want to do?

Specify a Name for the department
Specify a Department ID
Add the specified department to the department list
Cancel creating a new department

To specify a name for the department

Enter the department name. Names may contain spaces and punctuation marks.

To specify a Department ID

Enter the department ID, if you use IDs for your departments. IDs may be numbers, an abbreviation of the department name, or any other short ID name that you prefer.

To add the specified department to the department list

After you have entered the Department Name and Department ID, click Save & Return.

To cancel creating a new department

Click Cancel at any time before you click Save & Return.
