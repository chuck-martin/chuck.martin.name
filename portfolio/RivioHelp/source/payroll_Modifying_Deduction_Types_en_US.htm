Modifying Deduction Types

To modify deduction types

  1. Click , then under Payroll, click Setup.
  2. Click Deductions.
  3. Make any desired changes. Click Add to add an additional deduction type.
  4. Click Save.

Note: For InterPay users, once you define your original deduction types, these fields become non-editable. If you need to make changes, contact InterPay directly.
