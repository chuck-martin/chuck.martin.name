Modifying Direct Deposit

The Direct Deposit tab on the Payroll Information page shows your current direct deposit information. You can make changes to the information here, such as banks, accounts, and amounts deposited.

Note: Just making changes in the online form will not make the changes to existing direct deposit. You must print out the result and take the form, with a canceled check, to your Payroll Administrator.

To modify direct deposit information

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click Direct Deposit.
  3. Make any desired changes.
  4. Click Save & Return.
