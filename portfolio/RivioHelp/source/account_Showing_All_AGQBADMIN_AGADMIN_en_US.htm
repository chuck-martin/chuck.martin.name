Showing All Accounts

You can filter the account list, making it more manageable, by hiding all accounts with a status of inactive. But you may need to see all of your accounts.

Note: You can perform this task only if you are not seeing inactive accounts in the account list.

To show all accounts

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Account List.
  2. Click Show All.
    Note: If inactive accounts are already being shown, the Show All button will be replaced with Hide Inactive.
