Viewing Today

Any time you are on any Calendar page, you can get to today's page with one click. To view today's events, you must be on the Day or Week view.

To view today

  • On any Calendar page, click the link (which shows today's date) on the right-hand side under Today.

Note: The Calendar view does not change. For example, if you are viewing the Month tab, when you click the Today link, you will be viewing the month that contains today.
