The Export Data option is available from , Employee Information, Reports. The Export Data function allows you to export data about all your employee records in the system, including pending and terminated employees. This section is organized according to the tabs of the Employee Records.

  • Personal has 25 columns or fields.
  • Work has 21 columns or fields.
  • Employment has 22 columns or fields.
  • Dependents has 23 columns or fields.
  • Emergency has 19 columns or fields.
  • Pay Rate has 12 columns or fields.
  • Performance Review has 14 columns or fields.

To export data from your employee records

  1. Click on the report type from the Export Data section. The report is generated as a file to be downloaded.
  2. Depending on your browser configuration, the file will be immediately opened in your spreadsheet program, or you will be offered the choice of opening it from its current location (on our system) or saving it as a file on your computer's hard drive. Save the file.
  3. If your report opens in a spreadsheet, click File > Save As, and pick a location and filename for the new file. Click Save.
