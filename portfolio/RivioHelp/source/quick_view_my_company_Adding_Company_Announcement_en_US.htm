Adding a Company Announcement

Company announcements appear on the Quick View page in the Desktop View area on the  tab of all users.

You must have System Administrator or HR Administrator access rights to add a company announcement.

To add a company announcement

  1. Click , then under Desktop View, click Quick View.
  2. Click Add Announcement.
  3. Enter the announcement information.
    The expiration date is the last date that the announcement will appear on the Quick View page. The default is one month from now.
  4. If you want email sent to all employees to inform them that a new announcement is posted, select Notify all employees via e-mail.
    Note: Only employees who have e-mail addresses entered into the system will receive this e-mail, and you'll see an error message if any employees do not have an e-mail address entered.
  5. Click Save & Return.
  6. If you want the announcement to be published to all users' Quick View pages, select Display on Quick View.
  7. If you want the announcement to be marked as new, select Display As New.
  8. Click Save & Return.
