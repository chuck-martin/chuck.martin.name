Viewing a Specific Day

You can view any day through the year 2015. To view the events of that day, you must be on the Day or Week view.

To view a specific day

  1. On any day, week, or month Calendar view, on the right-hand side under Today, select a month, day, and year from the drop-down lists.
  2. Click Go.

Note: The Calendar view does not change. For example, if you are viewing the Month tab, when you click Go, you will be viewing the month that contains that day with that day highlighted.
