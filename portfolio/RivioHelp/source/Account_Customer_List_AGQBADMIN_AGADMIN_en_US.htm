Using your Customer List

The Customer List shows you a list of customers and projects. From here you can create new customers and projects or modify existing customers and projects.

The Customer List is available whether the QuickBooks® Interface is on or off. When the QuickBooks Interface is on, you can import your Customer and project records from QuickBooks and use that data anywhere you would use the accounts. QuickBooks Interface users can also create new customers and projects. These changes can then be exported to QuickBooks to keep both systems synchronized.

What do you want to do?

Add a customer
Add a project
View details of a specific customer
Show or Hide inactive customers
De-activate an active customer or activate an inactive customer

To add a customer

Click Add Customer. You go to the Customer Detail page to enter the customer information.

To add a project

  1. Select the parent customer or project. The Add Sub-Account button appears.
  2. Click Add Project. You go to the Project Detail page to enter the project information.

To view details of a specific customer

  1. Select the customer or project.
  2. Click View Selected Item. You go to the Customer Detail or Project Detail page to view or modify the information.

To show or hide inactive customers and projects

  • If inactive customers are currently shown, the button says Hide Inactive. Click it to hide inactive customers.
  • If active customers are currently shown, the button says Show Inactive. Click it to show inactive customers.

To de-activate an active customer and project or activate an inactive customer and project

  1. Select the customer or project.
  2. Click View Selected Item. You go to the Customer Detail or Project Detail page.
  3. Click Status Active or Inactive.
  4. Click Save.
