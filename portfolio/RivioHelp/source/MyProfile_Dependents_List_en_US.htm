The Dependents list shows a summary of your dependents. The summary includes the relationship, whether they are eligible for benefits, and whether they are an emergency contact for you. From this list you can add or modify details for an individual dependent.

What do you want to do?

Add a dependent
View or modify the information about an existing dependent
Delete one or more of the dependent entries in the list

To add a dependent

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Personal.
  2. Click Dependents
  3. Click Add Record.
  4. Enter the information about the dependent.
  5. Click Save & Return.

To view or change the information about an existing dependent

  1. Click the name of the dependent in the Dependent column. This will open the Dependent Information Form.
  2. Make any desired changes.
  3. Click Save & Return.

To delete one or more of the dependent entries in the list

  1. Select the check box next to the entries to be deleted.
  2. Click Delete.
