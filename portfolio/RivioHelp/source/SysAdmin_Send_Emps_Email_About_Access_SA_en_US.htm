The e-mail notice tells the employee how to sign in to the system. If the employee has already signed in to the system at least once, no notice will be automatically sent.

To send employees an e-mail notice that they now have access to the system

  1. Click the Access tab.
  2. Click the Select box to identify the employee or employees.
  3. Click Notify Employee.
