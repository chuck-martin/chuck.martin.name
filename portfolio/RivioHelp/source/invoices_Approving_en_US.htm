Approving an Invoice

An invoice can be submitted to you for approval for two reasons:

  • One of your coworkers has created an invoice to send to a client, and your company requires that it be approved before being sent out.
  • One of your coworkers has received an invoice, and your company requires that it be approved before payment can be made.

To approve an invoice that will be sent to a client

  1. Open an invoice with a status of Pending Approval.
  2. Click Forward.
  3. If someone else will be sending the invoice, select the user's name in the Select Name drop-down list.
    -or-
    If you want to sent the invoice to the client directly, then in the E-mail Address box, type a valid e-mail address.
  4. If desired, type an additional comment or Take Action by date.
  5. Click Send.

If you're a client and you received an e-mail that says you've been sent an invoice, the invoice contains a link to the Invoices area with a guest account that will let you access and work with that invoice.

To approve an invoice for payment

  1. Click the link in the e-mail you received. Your default browser will open to the sign-in page with the Company ID and User ID fields already filled in.
  2. In the Password field, type the word password.
  3. Click Sign-In.
  4. Click Approve and Submit for Payment.
  5. In the E-mail Address field, enter the e-mail address of the person who will pay the invoice.
  6. Click Send.
