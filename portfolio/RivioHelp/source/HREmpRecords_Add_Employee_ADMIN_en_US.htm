Adding an Employee

You can add an employee in two ways: the quick method and the payroll method. With the quick method, you enter just the basic, essential information about the employee (name, email address, phone number, employment status). With the payroll method, you enter all the information that is essential for payroll processing.

You must have System Administrator, Payroll Administrator, or HR Administrator access rights to add an employee.

To add an employee: quick method

  1. Click  , then under Employee Records, click Setup.
  2. Click Add Employee(s) Wizard - Quick Version.
  3. In the Add Employee wizard, enter the basic information about the employee.
  4. Click Save & Return.
    If you selected Yes to the "Would you like to use this employee for Payroll?" question, you will be taken to the Add Employee(s) Wizard - Payroll version to enter the employee's payroll information.

Note: You can also use the Quick Version of the Add Employee wizard by clicking the Add Employee link on the Employee Information list page.

To add an employee: payroll method

  1. Click  , then under Employee Records, click Setup.
  2. Click Add Employee(s) Wizard - Payroll Version.
  3. In the Payroll Employee Setup wizard, enter the information about the employee.
    Click Next when you have completed filling out each page.
  4. Click Save & Return.
