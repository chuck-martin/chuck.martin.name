The Emergency Contacts Information page records information about your emergency contacts. It may be seen and modified by you or an HR Administrator. From this list you can add or modify details for an individual emergency contact.

The Comments area lets you make notes that are only visible to others with HR Administrator privileges.

What do you want to do?

Identify this Emergency Contact as the primary emergency contact
Identify this Emergency Contact as also a dependent
Use the employee's home address for this emergency contact
Delete this entire entry

To identify this Emergency Contact as the primary emergency contact

  1. Select the Primary Contact check box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To identify this Emergency Contact as also a dependent

  1. Select the Listed as Dependent check box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To use the same home address as the employee

  1. Select the Use same home address as employee check box.
  2. Click Save & Return.

To delete this entire entry

  • Click Delete.
