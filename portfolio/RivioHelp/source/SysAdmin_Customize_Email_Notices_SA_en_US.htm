Customizing the System Access E-mail Notices

When you automatically notify employees of their new User IDs and passwords, two e-mail notices are sent. One contains your Company ID and the employee's User ID; the second contains the employee's temporary password.

You must have System Administrator access rights to customize e-mail notices.

To customize the e-mail sent when access rights are modified

  1. Click  , then under System Admin, click Access Rights.
  2. Click Notify Employee/Change Access Rights.
  3. Click Modify Welcome E-Mail.
  4. Change the From name or add or modify any text after the standard text.
  5. Click Save.
