Starting Direct Deposit

Note: Just filling out and saving the online form will not start direct deposit. You must print out the result (as described below) and take the form, with a canceled check, to your Payroll Administrator.

To start direct deposit

  1. Click My Work, then under My Profile, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click Direct Deposit.
  3. Select Use the following account(s) for direct deposit.
  4. Fill in the bank and account information in the Direct Deposit Account section.
  5. Click Save.
  6. Click Printer Friendly Format.
  7. Click Print Page.
