Modifying an Employee's Information

You can modify two types of employee information: basic information, which includes personal, dependent, employment, and emergency contact information, and payroll information, which includes earnings, deductions, and W-4 information.

You must have System Administrator, Payroll Administrator, or HR Administrator access rights to modify an employee's information.

To modify an employee's basic information

  1. Click  , then under Employee Records, click Employee Info.
  2. Click the name of the employee.
  3. Make any desired changes.
    The Employee Information page is divided into several tabs. To open each part, click the corresponding tab.
    Note: When you move to a new tab, the system automatically performs a Save of the information you have entered on the previous tab. Most tabs have some minimum required information that you must enter before you can move to another tab. For example, on the Personal tab, you must enter the employee's first and last name before you can move to the other tabs. If you move to a new tab without entering that minimum information, you stay on the original tab and see a warning message at the top of the page.
  4. Click Save & Return.
