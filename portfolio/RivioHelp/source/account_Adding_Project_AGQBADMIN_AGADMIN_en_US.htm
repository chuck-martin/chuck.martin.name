Adding a Project

Projects are associated with specific customers.

To add a project

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Customer List.
  2. Select a customer.
  3. Click Add Project.
    The Bill to Address fields are filled in, but the rest of the Contact Information fields are not because your contact for the project might be a different person at the company than the contact person for the company.
  4. Enter a Project Name and any other customer information. More information
  5. Click Save.
