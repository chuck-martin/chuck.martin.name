Modifying Access Rights

Access rights control which parts of the system are available to users and are controlled using roles. You can assign more than one role to an individual user. A user's roles determine which major items in the left menu the user can open.

You must have System Administrator access rights to modify access rights for other employees.

To modify access rights

  1. Click  , then under System Admin, click Access Rights.
  2. Click Notify Employee/Change Access Rights.
  3. Click a name.
  4. Select or clear one or more levels of access rights.
  5. Click Save.
