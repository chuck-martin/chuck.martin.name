Project Detail page

Use the following information to enter the correct information on the Project Detail page when you are adding or modifying a project.

Item

Description

Project Detail

The Project Name is required and what appears in the Customer List.

The Status can be Active or Inactive; you can hide Inactive projects in the Customer List.

Contact Information

Enter optional contact information for the person at the customer's company who is the contact for this project, including name, e-mail address, phone and fax numbers, and billing and shipping addresses. For Country, you can choose U.S.A. or Outside U.S.A.
