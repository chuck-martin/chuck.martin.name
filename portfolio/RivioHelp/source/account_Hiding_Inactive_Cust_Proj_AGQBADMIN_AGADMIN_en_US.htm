Hiding Inactive Customers and Projects

You can hide customers and projects that have a status of Inactive, making the list more manageable.

To hide inactive customers and projects

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Customer List.
  2. Click Hide Inactive.
    Note: If inactive customers and partners are already hidden, the Hide Inactive button will be replaced with Show All.
