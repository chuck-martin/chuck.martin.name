Showing All Customers and projects

You can filter the customers and project list, making it more manageable, by hiding all customers and projects with a status of inactive. But you may also need to view all your customers and projects.

Note: You can perform this task only if you are not seeing inactive customers and projects in the list.

To show all customers and projects

  1. Click , then under Accounting, click Customer List.
  2. Click Show All.
    Note: The Show All button is not available if you are already viewing the entire list of accounts.If inactive customers and partners are already being shown, the Show All button will be replaced with Hide Inactive.
