Signing In

Sign-in information was included in the e-mail notice that your System Administrator can send you when they set up your access to the system. By signing in, you securely identify your company and yourself.

You can also have your computer remember your ID or get a new password during sign-in.

To sign in

  1. Enter your identification information in three fields.
    All three of these fields are required to uniquely identify you:
    • Company ID. This identifies your company.
    • User ID. This identifies you, within your company.
    • Password. This confirms that you are who you say you are.
  2. Click Sign In.

To have your computer remember your IDs

  • Select the Remember IDs box.

Note: Your browser will record your Company ID and your User ID in a "cookie," a small file that your browser will show only to our system. The next time you come to this sign-in page, those items will be filled in for you. All you need to do at that point is fill in your password.

For security, do not use this option on a computer you share with others.

To get a new password during sign-in

  • Click Forgot your password?

Note: If this is the first time you are signing in, this option will not be available to you.
