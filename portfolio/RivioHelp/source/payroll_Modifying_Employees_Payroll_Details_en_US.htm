Modifying an Employee's Payroll Details

You can modify an employee's payroll details, including wage information and deductions.

You must have System Administrator or Payroll Administrator access rights to modify an employee's payroll details.

To modify an employee's payroll details

  1. Click  , then under Employee Records, click Payroll Info.
  2. Click the name of the employee.
  3. Make any desired changes.
    Click the tabs to access the different types of information.
  4. Click Save & Return.
