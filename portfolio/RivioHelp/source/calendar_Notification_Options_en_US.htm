Notification Options

You can let the people in your company know when you are at a scheduled event and unavailable. You can also add contact information if you can be reached in other ways than normal (such as through your cell phone instead of your office phone). That notification is set up in this section under Company notification via employee locator and then appears in the Employee Locator in  >  > Employee Locator.

  • Select Notify entire company of your out of office status if you want to be listed in the Employee Locator for this event's time. Then enter a reason, end date, and any additional optional comments.
  • Select Reachable if you want to add information about how you can be reached during this event. Then enter that information.
