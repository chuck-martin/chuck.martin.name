About Portals

Portals are pre-defined collections of views that allow you to see useful sets of data. The views in each portal are defined by the Workgroup Administrator. You can configure the available views in each portal.

The views in a Portal are similar to those in a Dashboard. The differences are:

  • In Portals, the views are active: You can click on the view title and go directly to that view in its Project InVision module.
  • In Dashboard, views can be cross-Workgroup, where in Portals, views are solely within one Workgroup.

Click on the link in the view title bar to go to that view in its Project InVision module.
