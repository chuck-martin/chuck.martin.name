Creating Risks

Risks can be associated with either the overall project or with specific Tasks in the Project. If a Risk is associated with a specific Task, then the Risk is displayed in the Time Tracking application so that time can be posted against the Risk and rolled up into the Task. See Time Tracking.

To create a Risk

  1. In the Projects list, select a Project.
  2. Under Modules, click Risks.
  3. Click  New. The New Risks Detail Form appears.
  4. Enter the Risk information. Fields with bold labels are required.
  5. Click Submit.
