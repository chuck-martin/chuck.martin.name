Project Scorecard

The Project Scorecard allows an organization to prioritize Projects based on weights given to specific aspects of the Project, based on the importance of those aspects to the organization, and then calculate a score for the Project.

To enter and calculate Scorecard information

  1. View a Projects Detail form.
  2. Under Pages, click Scorecard.
  3. Enter numbers in each field based on your company's rating scale to rate the importance of each item.
  4. Click  Save.
  5. Click  Refresh. All calculated fields in the Scorecard are updated.

Example

A sample Project Scorecard has 5 criteria. Weighting percentages total 100 percent and your company uses a criteria rating scale of 1 to 5. The formula for this Scorecard is:

(Strategic Alignment x 30%) + (ROI x 20%) + (Technology Fit x 20%) + (Business Unit Priority x 20%) + (Difficulty x 10%) = Total

You enter:

  • Strategic Alignment: 3
  • ROI: 3
  • Technology Fit: 2
  • Business Use Priority: 5
  • Difficulty: 5

The resulting calculation: (3 x 30%) + (3 x 20%) + (2 x 20%) + (5 x 20%) + (5 x 10%) = 3.4
