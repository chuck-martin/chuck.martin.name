Changing Your Password

When your User Login ID is first created in Project InVision, the Workgroup Administrator sets your initial password. If you have been granted the proper security permissions, you are able to change your password.

If you use NT authentication in Project InVision as your login protocol, you are not able to change your password. Your System Administrator handles password changes to NT accounts.

To change your password

  1. Click My Setup. The My Setup window appears.
  2. Under Select Item, click Change Password.
  3. Enter your current password (once, in the Current Password field) and your new password (twice, in the New Password and Confirm Password fields).
  4. Click  Save.
