About Templates

Templates are used in Project InVision to ensure that a consistent Project Management approach is maintained by using Templates. This ensures that every assigned Resource is providing information consistently across the Enterprise or across a Workgroup.

For example, Templates ensure that Status Reports are completed in the same way so that Executives can compare Projects based on data presented in the same format.
