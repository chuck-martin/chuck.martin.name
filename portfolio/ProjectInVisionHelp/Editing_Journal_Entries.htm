Editing Journal Entries

Journal entries can be edited at any time.

You can edit Journal entries only if you have the proper security.

To edit a Journal entry

  1. View a Journal Detail form.
  2. Make any desired changes. Click the links under Pages to see more information.
  3. Click  Save.

Discussion entries entered in any of the modules are actually captured as Journal entries in the Journals module. In addition to editing the items in the Journals module, you can also reply to items when you're in the Central Discussions View. See About Discussions.
