Documents Module Views

Default Views have been created for the Documents module. Your security access determines what Views you can see.

Reports may be customized by the Workgroup Administrator or a Report Writer, so some View names or fields may not exist or may be labeled differently than shown here.

-Standard-

This View shows general information about selected documents. You can view and edit Document information, such as Document Title, Association, Revision Date, Status, Author, Comments, Project Number, and Project Title.

Basic View

This View shows basic information about selected documents, including Type, Status and Workflow of the documents assigned to a Project. Fields: Document Number, Document Name, Type, Status, and Workflow.

Date View

This View shows date information about selected documents: when a document was entered into the system, when it was last revised, and when it is due. Fields: Document Name, Revised Date, Author, Document Number, Date Entered, and Due Date.
