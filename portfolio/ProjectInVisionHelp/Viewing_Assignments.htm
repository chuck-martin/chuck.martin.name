Viewing Assignments

The Assignments View shows your Tasks and Task-related items (Issues, Defects, Risks, Changes, Requirements, etc.), as well as non-Project activities. Assignments are presented in an outline format grouped first by Workgroup, then by Project, and then by Task-related items appear under their associated Task so that time can be posted against them and rolled up into the Task.

You can view all your open assignments that start before a specified date (the default view) or all your assignments (open and completed) between two specified dates. For a Task to be considered open, it must have Remaining Hours for time to be posted against.

To view open assignments

  1. In the Display drop-down list at the top of the Assignments View, select Before.
  2. Click  Calendar next to the Before date. The Select Date dialog box appears.
  3. Select a date. (Click the arrows at the end of the scroll bar to move by month. Click in the scroll bar to move by year.)
  4. Click  Go.

To view all assignments between two dates

  1. In the Display drop-down list at the top of the Assignments View, select Between.
  2. Click  Calendar next to each date. The Select Date dialog box appears.
  3. Select a date in each. (Click the arrows at the end of the scroll bar to move by month. Click in the scroll bar to move by year.)
  4. Click  Go.
