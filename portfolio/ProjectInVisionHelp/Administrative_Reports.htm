Administrative Reports

Administrative Reports contain recapping information related to the system and user status. The Reports available in the Administrative section are described below (with Report Options), along with a description of the Report and the fields that are captured.

Reports may be customized by the Workgroup Administrator or a Report Writer, so some fields may not exist or may be labeled differently than shown here.

Users Current

This Report shows Resources who are currently logged in to Project InVision, and the length of time that they have been logged in.

Users Login Status

This Report shows all users of Project InVision and whether they are logged in or not, the amount of time that users have been logged in, and the length of time since logged out users last logged in.
