Removing Workflows

The process for removing a workflow from a record is the same for all types of records where workflows can be applied. Workflows can be applied to records in:

  • Projects
  • Documents
  • Issues
  • Defects
  • Risks
  • Changes
  • Requirements

When a Workflow is removed, the record Status reverts to its default initial Status.

If a Workflow is required (the field label is in bold), it may not be removed.

To remove a workflow

  1. View the Detail Form for the record.
  2. If the Information page is not displayed, click Information.
  3. In the Workflow drop-down list, select the blank choice at the top of the list.
  4. Click Submit.
