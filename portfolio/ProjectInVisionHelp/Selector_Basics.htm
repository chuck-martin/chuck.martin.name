Selector Basics

The Selector is a hierarchy, like an outline, of your Projects, showing them grouped in different ways. The view on the right, whether a list, a chart, a graph, a report, or anything else, is controlled (and possibly limited) by what's selected in the Selector.

Expand and collapse individual sections of the Selector hierarchy be clicking on the + (to expand) or - (to collapse) next to any item that contains other items.

Limit the levels you view by clicking in the numbers at the top of the Selector. for example, click 2 to view just the first 2 levels.
