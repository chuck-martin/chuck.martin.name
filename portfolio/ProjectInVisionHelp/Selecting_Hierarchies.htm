Selecting Hierarchies

Multiple hierarchies can be defined (by the Workgroup Administrator) to organize your Projects and Resources. Hierarchies group records into groups and sub-groups. Common hierarchies could include Location and Organizational.

To select a hierarchy

  1. If it is not open, click  Selector to view the Selector pane.
  2. In the drop-down list at the top of the pane, select a hierarchy.
