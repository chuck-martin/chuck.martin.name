Approving Timesheets

Timesheet approval is done on an assignment-by-assignment basis so you can review the hours submitted for each Task, Task-related item, and non-Project activity. You approve timesheets for one week and in only one Workgroup at a time.

To approve Timesheets

  1. If you are not on the Approve Time page, click the down arrow next to the View selection in the Navigation Menu, then select Approval Screen.
  2. Click on the down arrow next to Workgroup and select a Workgroup.
  3. Click on the down arrow next to Filter By to apply an appropriate Filter to the list.
  4. Select a week from the Week of calendar. (Click  Calendar next to the Week Of field, then select a date from the Select Date dialog box.)
    The date displayed is the first day of the selected week.
  5. Click in the Approve column for each Task or Task-related item, then from the drop-down list, select Approve.
  6. Click  Submit Approval.
  7. Click Yes.
