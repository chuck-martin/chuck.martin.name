Changing Time Increments

The Daily View is a graphical chart view where you can click on time increments to enter Actual Work Hours throughout the day. This view can show time in increments of quarter-hour, half-hour, and full-hour.

To change time increments in the Daily View

  • Click  View Options , then select Time Increment, then Hourly, Half Hourly, or Quarter Hourly.

The view refreshes automatically.
