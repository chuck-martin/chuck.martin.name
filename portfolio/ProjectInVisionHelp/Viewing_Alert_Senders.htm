Viewing Alert Senders

If an Alert was sent by another Project InVision user (as opposed to an automatically generated Alert), you can view information about that user.

To view who sent an alert

  1. View an alert that was sent by another Project InVision user.
    The Sent By field of such Alerts is a blue underlined link.
  2. Click the name in the Sent By field.

The Resources Detail Form for that user appears.
