Editing Availability

Availability defines when a Resource is and is not available for work on Project and Task activities. Here you define when a Resource is scheduled for Non-Project Activities and when a Resource is scheduled to become available. Non-Project Activities are any time, both work-related (such as training or administrative work) and non-work-related (such as vacation or PTO) where you are not available for project work.

To add a Non-Project Activity

  1. View a Resources Detail Form.
  2. Under Pages, click Availability.
  3. In the Non-Project Activities list, click an empty row.
  4. Enter the Non-Project Activity information.
    1. In the Non-Project Activity field, select an item from the drop-down list.
    2. In the Description field, enter details about the activity.
    3. In the Units field, enter a percentage (use a decimal between 0.0 and 1.0) of your time that you will be involved in this activity.
    4. In the Start and Finish fields, click  Calendar, then click on the start and end dates. (Click the arrows at the end of the scroll bar to move by month. Click in the scroll bar to move by year.) The Work in hours will be automatically calculated.
  5. Click  Save.

Some Non-Project Activity, such as vacation, flows into your time sheet. Updating your Non-Project activity is important to keep your time sheet information current.

To update Non-Project Activity

  1. View a Resources Detail Form.
  2. Under Pages, click Availability.
  3. In the Non-Project Activities list, click in the Actual Work field of the activity.
  4. Press F2
  5. Enter the hours you've spent on the activity.
  6. Click  Save.

Availability Profile information defines when you are going to be available a different amount of time than normal. Examples might be 0% for 12 weeks for maternity leave or 75% because you're moving from full time to part time.

In cases where the availability change is temporary, you must add 2 items, one for the change from regular and one for the change back to regular.

To add an Availability Profile item

If no Availability Profile information is defined for a Resource, they are assumed to be available 100%.

  1. View a Resources Detail Form.
  2. Under Pages, click Availability.
  3. In the Availability Profile list, click an empty row.
  4. Enter the Availability Profile information.
    1. In the Effective Date field, click  Calendar, then click on the start date. (Click the arrows at the end of the scroll bar to move by month. Click in the scroll bar to move by year.)
    2. In the Availability field, enter a percentage of your regular time that you will be available.
  5. Click  Save.
