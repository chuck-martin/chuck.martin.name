About the Resources Module

The Project InVision Resources module is where you manage information about and categorize the resources in your organization. Resources are the people in your company who have skills and experience and who can be assigned to Projects to work on Tasks. They can post Actual Work Hours against these Tasks so that the Task and Project information is updated.

Resources are maintained at the enterprise level and are granted membership to various Workgroups so that they can be assigned to Projects and Tasks. Only Resources who are members of a current Workgroup are displayed in the Resources module and can be assigned to Projects in that Workgroup. Project InVision tracks the detailed work assignments of Resources across all Projects, allowing an enterprise to better understand and optimize the use of critical resources. You can view Resource information in list views or in the Resources Detail Form.
