About Workgroups

Workgroups are named groups that are used like teams to organize projects with similar methodology and business practices. Each Workgroup has similar terminology, procedures, pull-down values, and user access. Users are assigned to one or more Workgroups, depending on the requirements of your organization, to work on projects.

If your company was a large filing cabinet, each drawer might represents a Workgroup, with each drawer filled with similar Project types and Project information. In that drawer, each file folder is an individual Project and contains all the information related to that Project: schedules, documents, resource records, issue records, change requests, etc.

When you log in, you are taken to the Workgroup where you were last working.
