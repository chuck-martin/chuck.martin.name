Time Tracking Reports

Time Tracking Reports contain information related to Work Hours posted by Resources. The Reports available for the Time Tracking application are described below (with Report Options), along with a description of the Report and the fields that are captured.

Reports may be customized by the Workgroup Administrator or a Report Writer, so some fields may not exist or may be labeled differently than shown here.

Timesheet Exception (Beginning Date)

This Report shows Resources who have not posted a Timesheet for the specified period. The Report lists the Workgroup, Week of the Report, and a list of the Resource names and numbers.

Timesheet (Resource, Beginning Date)

This Report shows the documented time worked by a Resource for a specified week. The Report shows the Workgroup and Resource, as well as the approved Timesheet entries along with their associated Projects, the Task or Task-Related item description, the Actual Work Hours per day, the Total Work Hours per week, the Remaining Work, Billing Code, and Cost Center. These numbers are then summarized by day and for the week.
