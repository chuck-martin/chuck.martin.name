Creating Journal Entries

Standalone Journal entries (that are not part of discussions) can be created at any time.

To create a Journal entry

  1. In a Projects list view, select a Project.
  2. Under Modules, click Journal.
  3. Click  New. The New Journal Detail Form appears.
  4. Enter the Journal information. Fields with bold labels are required.
  5. Click  Save.

Standard Journal records do not appear in the Central Discussions View of the Journals module.
