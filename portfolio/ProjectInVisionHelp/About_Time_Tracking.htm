About Time Tracking

The Project InVision Time Tracking application is where Resources working on projects submit weekly Timesheets and where managers approve those Timesheets. Resources can post time (actual hours worked) against their assignments to Tasks or task-related items, such as Issues, Defects, Risks, Changes, or Requirements and time spent on non-Project activities, such as administrative work, sick leave, training, or vacation. When a Resource submits a weekly Timesheet, the time entries are routed for approval, then synchronized with the projects in Project InVision and any associated project schedule files.

You can log in directly to the Time Tracking application from the login page, or switch to the Time Tracking application from within any other Project InVision application.
