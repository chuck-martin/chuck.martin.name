Editing Contact Information

Contact information for a Resource includes an address, telephone numbers, and email addresses, and is stored on the Contact Info page of the Resource Detail Form. Multiple phone numbers and email addresses can be stored here.

To add Contact Info information

  1. View a Resources Detail form.
  2. Under Pages, click Contact Info.
  3. Click in an empty field in the Phone Type column, then select a type from the drop-down list.
  4. Click in the corresponding field in the Phone column, then enter a phone number.
  5. Repeat steps 1 and 2 for additional phone numbers
  6. Click in an empty field in the Email Type column, then select a type from the drop-down list.
  7. Click in the corresponding field in the email column, then enter an email address.
  8. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for additional email addresses.
  9. Click in the Address box and enter Address information.
  10. Click  Save.

To edit Contact Info information

  1. View a Resources Detail form.
  2. Under Pages, click Contact Info.
  3. Click in the phone number or email field you want to change.
  4. Press F2.
  5. Enter the changes.
  6. Click  Save.

To delete Contact Info information

  1. View a Resources Detail form.
  2. Under Pages, click Contact Info.
  3. Click in the phone number or email field you want to change.
  4. Press Delete.
  5. Click  Save.
