Entering Actual Work Hours

In the Time Tracking application you apply Actual Work Hours to Tasks, Task-related items (Issues, Defects, Risks, Changes, and Requirements), and non-Project activities. Hours applied to Task-related items are rolled up to their associated Task. You can enter Actual Hours in the Timesheet View or the Daily View.

To enter Actual Work Hours in the Timesheet View

Before you can add Actual Work Hours in a Timesheet, you must add the Assignments to the Timesheet View.

  1. Click the assignment row in Timesheet View where you want to assign hours.
  2. Enter your Actual Work Hours for each day of the week. Click in each field and enter a number. As you enter hours, the Remaining Hours estimate is automatically updated.
    Time is recorded in hourly increments, so 15 minutes would be entered as 0.25 hours.
  3. Click  Save.

If you entered more Actual Work Hours for a Task than allotted, the Remaining Hours becomes a negative number and you cannot save the timesheet. You must either click in the Remaining Hours fields and change the number to 0 (zero), indicating that the Task is complete, or to a positive number, indicating that additional hours are added to the estimate and there is additional work to be performed. You should add a note to the Timesheet assignment to record the estimate change.

Once you've completed entering hours for the week, you can submit the Timesheet for approval.

To enter Actual Work Hours in the Daily View

Before you can add Actual Work Hours in a Timesheet, you must add the Assignments to the Timesheet View pane.

  1. Click the assignment row in Timesheet View where you want to assign hours.
  2. In the Daily View, click the time blocks for the hours you've worked. Each block fills with color and the description of the Task. The hours are also posted in the Timesheet View.
    You can change the time increments in the daily view to quarter-hour, half-hour, or full-hour.
  3. Click  Save.

If you entered more Actual Work Hours for a Task than allotted, the Remaining Hours becomes a negative number and you cannot save the timesheet. You must either click in the Remaining Hours fields and change the number to 0 (zero), indicating that the Task is complete, or to a positive number, indicating that additional hours are added to the estimate and there is additional work to be performed. You should add a note to the Timesheet assignment to record the estimate change.

Once you've completed entering hours for the week, you can submit the Timesheet for approval.
