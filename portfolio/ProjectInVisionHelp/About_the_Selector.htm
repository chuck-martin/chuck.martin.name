About the Selector

The Selector is a hierarchy, like an outline, of your Projects, showing them grouped in different ways. The view on the right, whether a list, a chart, a graph, a report, or anything else, is controlled (and possibly limited) by what's selected in the Selector.

A hierarchy is the arrangement of informational elements from general to specific. Appearing as a tree-like structure with various branches appearing under each other, Hierarchies reveal top-level groupings with sub-level categories listed beneath them. These items then fall to a bottom level where individual Projects are linked to the top-level items.

In Project InVision, hierarchies allow for Project numbers such as costing or billing information to be rolled up by hierarchical level.
