Editing Resources

Resource information may change over time. You can change Resource information at any time, and that information will be available across multiple Workgroups.

You can edit Resources only if you have the proper security.

To edit a Resource

  1. View a Resources Detail Form.
  2. Make any desired changes. Fields with bold labels are required. Click the links under Pages to enter more information.
  3. Click Submit.
