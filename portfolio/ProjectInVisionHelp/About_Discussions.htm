About Discussions

Discussions are threaded conversations between Resources involved with a particular record. Once a Discussions message has been created, other Resources can then respond to that message and voice their opinion on the matter. You can post new topics in Discussions and respond either publicly or privately to existing Discussion messages. Discussions can be used for informational issues or questions and you can use them to maintain a conversation across geographically-dispersed areas. Discussions are available in the following modules: Projects, Tasks, Issues, Defects, Journals, Risks, Changes, and Requirements.
