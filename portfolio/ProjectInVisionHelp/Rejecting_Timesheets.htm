Rejecting Timesheets

Timesheet rejection is done on a task-by-task basis so you can review the hours submitted for each Task and Task-related item. You reject timesheets for one week and in only one Workgroup at a time.

To reject a timesheet

  1. If you are not on the Approve Time page, click the down arrow next to the View selection in the Navigation Menu, then select Approval Screen.
  2. Click on the down arrow next to Workgroup and select a Workgroup.
  3. Click on the down arrow next to Filter By to apply an appropriate Filter to the list.
  4. Select a week from the Week of calendar. (Click  Calendar next to the Week Of field, then select a data from the Select Date dialog box.)
    The date displayed is the first day of the selected week.
  5. Click in the Approve column for each Task or Task-related item, then from the drop-down list, select Reject.
  6. Optionally add a note explaining the rejection:
    1. Click in the Notes column for the Task or Task-related item.
    2. Click the ellipsis button.
    3. Enter the note text.
    4. Click Submit.
  7. Click  Submit Approval.
  8. Click Yes.
