Viewing Information

You can organize the information you view in Project InVision in many ways. In addition to the many report and chart views, the list views can be easily filtered, sorted, and grouped. Filtering limits the records you view based on specific criteria, sorting can be done on multiple levels, and grouping clusters records. All these viewing options tie in with the Selector.

List views show an incremental amount of records at a time, an increment that can be set by your Workgroup Administrator (the default is 20). At the bottom of the list view are three links, Refresh, More, and All. Click More to add the next incremental amount of records or click All to view all records (this may take a long time to display if there are many records). Click Refresh to redisplay the records in their default view: the initial incremental amount and column widths.
