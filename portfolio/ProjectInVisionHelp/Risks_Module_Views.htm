Risks Module Views

Default Views have been created for the Risks module. Your security access determines what Views you can see.

Reports may be customized by the Workgroup Administrator or a Report Writer, so some View names or fields may not exist or may be labeled differently than shown here.

-Standard-

This View shows current status information for selected Project Tasks: the individual Tasks, the Current Finish date of the Task, and the % of the Task that is complete. You can view and edit information related to Actual and Baseline Dates, as well as comments and Resource information. Fields: Project Number, Project Title, Task Name, Task Number, Current Finish, % Complete, Resource Initials, Actual Start, Actual Finish, Base Start, Base Finish, Current Start, Comments, Task Code, and Resource Name.

Detail

This View shows detailed information about Risks that exist for selected Projects, grouped by Project. You can see information related to the Risk, as well as who is responsible for it, when it was evaluated, and the current status of the Risk. Fields: Project Number, Project Title, Risk Number, Risk Title, Responsible, Submitted by, Date Entered, Evaluation Date, Date Closed, Status, Probability, Magnitude, Type, Priority, Description, Mitigation Plan, and Impact.

Risk Actual Work Pie

This View is a Pie Chart with slices based on Rollup selection and the Date Range specified. Depending on the Rollup used, the Work Hours will display for the specified Date Range, grouped by Rollup. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project. Fields: Risk Number, Risk Title, Status, Actual Work, and Timesheet Date.

Risk Count Chart

This View shows a total count of Risks separated into groups by Rollup. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project.

Risk Count Pie

ThisView is a pie chart with slices based on Rollup selection. Depending on the rollup used, the counts will display grouped by the various values that exist for the Rollup field. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project. Fields: Risk Number, Risk Title, Status, and Count.

Risk Personal Count Chart

This View shows a total count of Risks separated into groups by Rollup and the number of Risks per Rollup group where the current user is the Responsible party. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project.

Risk Priority Matrix

This View shows a matrix that compares Risk Magnitude to Risk Priority. It separates the counts into High, Medium, and Low magnitude versus High, Medium, and Low Priorities.

Risk Probability Matrix

This View shows a matrix that compares Risk Probability to Risk Magnitude. It separates the counts into High, Medium, and Low Probability versus High, Medium, and Low Magnitude.

Risk Trend Chart

This View shows how Risks have progressed over time.
