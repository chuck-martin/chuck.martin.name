Adding Assignments to Timesheets

You must add assignments (Tasks, Task-related items, such as Issues, Defects, Risks, Changes, or Requirements, and non-Project activities) to a Timesheet before you can apply Actual Hours to those assignments.

If you have the proper security, you will see a Find button next to the Resource name and can add assignments for other Resources. Click Find to select a different Resource.

To add Assignments to a Timesheet

  1. Select a week from the Week of calendar. (Click  Calendar next to the Week Of field, then select a data from the Select Date dialog box.)
    The date displayed is the first day of the selected week.
  2. View your assignments, then in the Assignments hierarchy, click an Assignment to select it. Click the  plus buttons to expand the hierarchy and see the Tasks associated with the Projects and the Task-related items associated with Tasks.
  3. Click  Add.
    You can also double-click the Assignment.
    The assignment is added to the Timesheet View for the currently selected week.
  4. Click  Save.

You can now enter Actual Work Hours for the Assignment.
