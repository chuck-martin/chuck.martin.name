About Dashboard

The Project InVision Dashboard application is where executives and managers obtain high-level status of a specific project or portfolio of projects. Dashboards may also be useful to Resources working on projects, providing a central place to review all project items.

A Dashboard is a collection of multiple Views, each offering different information related to the project or portfolio, where you can view specific information in a quick snapshot format. Each Dashboard view gives information about a Project or Project-related Item, or a group of Projects or Project-related Items. You can zoom in and zoom out of the Views to see more in-depth or more general information.

Dashboards are defined by the Workgroup Administrator and then distributed to individual Users.
