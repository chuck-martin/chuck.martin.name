Printing Timesheets

You can print both the Daily View and the Timesheet View of a weekly Timesheet.

To print the Timesheet View

  1. Click  Output, then select Print Timesheet View. A print preview window appears.
  2. Click  Print.

To print the Daily View

  1. Click  Output, then select Print Daily View. A print preview window appears.
  2. Click  Print.
