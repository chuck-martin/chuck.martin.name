Changing Workgroups

In the Portfolio Manager application, you can view information within just one Workgroup at a time. That Workgroup is shown as the first item in the Navigation menu.

To change workgroups

  1. In the Navigation menu, click the down arrow next to the name of the workgroup.
  2. Select a workgroup.

The list of Workgroups includes only those Workgroups you have been assigned to. Your security rights may be different in each Workgroup.
