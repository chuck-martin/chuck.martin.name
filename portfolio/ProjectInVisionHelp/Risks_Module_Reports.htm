Risks Module Reports

Risks module Reports contain information related to Risks and the Status of Risks across the Enterprise. The Reports available for the Risks module are described below (with Report Options), along with a description of the Report and the fields that are captured.

Reports may be customized by the Workgroup Administrator or a Report Writer, so some fields may not exist or may be labeled differently than shown here.

Risks List - Details (Projects, Risks Filter)

This Report shows detailed information about Risks, grouped by Project. Project fields: Project Number, Project Title, Risk Number, Risk Title, Responsible, Submitted by, Date Entered, Evaluation Date, Date Closed, Status, Probability, Magnitude, Type, Priority, Description, Mitigation Plan, and Impact.

Risks List - Status (Projects, Risks Filter)

This Report shows all Risk, grouped by Project. Project fields: Project Number, Project Title, Risk Number, Risk Title, Responsible, Submitted by, Status, Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Date Entered, Evaluation Date, and Date Closed.

Risks Count (Projects, Risks Filter, Rollup)

This Report shows a total count of Risks separated into groups by Rollup. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project.

Risks Count - My Risks (Projects, Risks Filter)

This Report shows a total count of your Risks by Rollup. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project.

Risks Count Pie (Projects, Risks Filter, Rollup)

This Report shows a Pie Chart with Risks by Rollup, plus a table that shows Risk details, grouped by Rollup. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project. Project fields: Risk Number, Risk Title, Status, and Count.

Risk Pie Chart - Actual Work (Projects, Risks Filter, Projects Filter, Rollup, Date Range, From Date, To Date)

This Report shows a Pie Chart with Work Hours by Rollup, plus a table that shows Work Hours details grouped by Rollup. Numbers can be rolled up by Type, Priority, Probability, Magnitude, Status, Responsible, Submitted by, or Project. Project fields: Risk Number, Risk Title, Status, Actual Work, and Timesheet Date.

Risks Matrix - Priority (Projects, Risks Filter)

This Report shows a matrix that compares Risk Magnitude to Risk Priority, comparing High, Medium, and Low for both.

Risks Matrix - Probability (Projects, Risks Filter)

This Report shows a matrix that compares Risk Probability to Risk Magnitude, comparing High, Medium, and Low for both.
