About Custom Modules

Custom modules can be created if other modules in Project InVision do not meet the exact needs of your organization. Data fields in the custom modules capture information specific to the custom module. There is no limit to the number of custom modules that can be created in Project InVision.

The Workgroup Administrator, based on the needs of the organization, adds new custom modules to the Enterprise as needed. The Workgroup Administrator also grants permissions for accessing any custom modules added to the Enterprise.
