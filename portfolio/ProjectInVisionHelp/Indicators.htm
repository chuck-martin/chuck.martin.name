Indicators

Record lists may have Views that show Indicators: icons that show record status. Double-click on any icon to view the corresponding page of the record Detail Form.

Indicator Name Indicates
< Condition Red Project is in critical Status and needs immediate attention.
▲ Condition Yellow Project has issues that may impact successful completion.
= Condition Green Project is on track.
Behind Schedule Item is behind schedule. Actual Finish Date is past Current Date.
Comments Comments exist for the record.
Completed Work Complete is 100% or Status is Closed.
Discussions Discussion items exist for the record.
High Priority Item is a high priority item.
Links Links exist for the record.
Over Budget Actual Costs exceed Baseline Costs.
Over Work Actual Work exceeds Baseline Work.
Unmatched Resources Resources that do not exist in the Resources module have been assigned to the Project.

Project Summary Indicators

In the Project Summary view, indicators are used for the status of budgets, schedules and entire projects. The formulas that define whether any status is Red, Yellow, or Green are defined by the Workgroup Administrator.

Indicator Name Indicates
< Condition Red Critical Status and needs immediate attention.
▲ Condition Yellow Issues that may impact successful completion.
= Condition Green Project, budget, or schedule is on track.
