About the Risks Module

The Project InVision Risks Module is where you manage and collaborate on project-related Risks as they arise during the course of a project. A Risk is a potential threat to the successful completion of a Project or Task. In the Risks Module, you track Risks from discovery through evaluation to mitigation. You can view Risk information in list views or in the Risks Detail Form.

Risks are identified during the development of a project plan and during the implementation of the project. Risks are identifiable potential problems that may affect the implementation of the project combined with the estimated probability that they will occur. Risks need to be identified so they can be planned for and managed if the events do occur. The Project InVision Risks module captures detailed information about Risks, including the Name, Description, Priority, Status, Workflow, Submitted by Resource, and Responsible Resource.

The natural progression of a Risk is to: (1) define the Risk and enter it into the application as a new Risk, (2) identify and assign a Responsible party and Evaluation Date, and (3) when the Risk is mitigated, enter the Resolution Date and Date Completed, and change the Status appropriately.
