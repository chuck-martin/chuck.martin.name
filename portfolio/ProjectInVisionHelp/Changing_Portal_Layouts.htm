Changing Portal Layouts

In any Portal, you can change how the views are laid out. You can choose which views are in the Portal (from all the views available in that Portal), how many columns of views, and what order to place the views.

To change a Portal layout

  1. When viewing a Portal, click  Layout. The Portal Layout window appears.
    • To change the number of columns of views, select 1, 2, or 3.
    • To remove a view (it will still be available to add), click the view to select it, then click Remove.
    • To add a view (if you have any to add), click the view in the Available Views list to select it, then click Add.
    • To move a view, click the view to select it, then click Left, Right, Up, or Down.
    • To resize the height of a view, click the bottom border of the view and drag it to the size you want.
  2. The sample views rearrange automatically after each selection. The red bar on the left margin of the sample views indicates the approximate limit of the browser window.
  3. Click Submit.
