Removing Assignments from Timesheets

When you remove Assignments (Tasks, Task-related items, and non-Project activities) from a Timesheet, they are removed for a specific week only.

If you have the proper security, you will see a Find button next to the Resource name and can remove assignments for other Resources. Click Find to select a different Resource.

To remove Assignments from a Timesheet

  1. Select a week from the Week of calendar. (Click  Calendar next to the Week Of field, then select a data from the Select Date dialog box.)
  2. In the Timesheet View, click an Assignment to select it.
  3. Click  Remove.
  4. Click  Save.
