About Workflows

Workflows are processes for Projects, Tasks, and other items that mimic your company's business logic. Workflows define the order of Statuses, when Document records are created, who can make changes at any Status, and more. Modules can have many Workflows, but a record in any module can be assigned only one Workflow.

The Workflow has a default initial Status and includes logic to control the process and changes of a Status by a user. For the Projects and Documents modules, required fields, non-editable fields, required Documents, and required Document Approvals are controlled by the record's status in the Workflow.

Once a Workflow has been applied, the choices available to in the Status drop-down list box at any time are dictated by the Workflow that was assigned.

Once a Status has been updated for a record based on the Workflow, unless the Workflow specifically allows it, you cannot go back to a previous Status. To revert record Status, contact the Workgroup Administrator.
