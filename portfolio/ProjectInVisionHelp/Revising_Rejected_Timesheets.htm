Revising Rejected Timesheets

When a Timesheet entry is rejected, it is sent back to you for revision. In the Timesheet View, rejected items show the icon in the Approve column.

To revise rejected Timesheets

  • Click on the item in the Timesheet View, then re-enter your Actual Work hours.

If the approver provided feedback in a note, you'll see the icon in the Approve column. Click in the field, then click the ellipsis button to view the note text.
